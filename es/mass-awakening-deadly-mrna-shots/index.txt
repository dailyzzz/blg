15:T3ab8,<h1 id="survey-confirms-mass-awakening-to-deadly-covid-19-mrna-shots">Survey Confirms Mass Awakening to Deadly COVID-19 mRNA Shots</h1>
<h2 id="the-ancient-pattern">The Ancient Pattern</h2>
<blockquote>
<p>"And the eyes of them both were opened." — Genesis 3:7</p>
</blockquote>
<p>The opening of eyes is one of the most powerful motifs in Scripture. In Genesis, Adam and Eve's eyes were opened to the knowledge of good and evil — an awakening that carried consequences they did not anticipate. In 2 Kings 6:17, Elisha prayed, "LORD, I pray thee, open his eyes, that he may see," and the young servant's eyes were opened to see the mountain full of horses and chariots of fire — the invisible army of God that had been there all along.</p>
<p>A mass awakening is underway. Eyes that were shut by fear, compliance, institutional trust, and social pressure are opening. Survey data now confirms what those with open eyes have observed for years: the public is awakening to the reality that COVID-19 mRNA shots are not safe, that they have caused death and serious injury on a massive scale, and that the institutions that promoted them betrayed the public trust.</p>
<p>The Confliction track <a href="https://confliction.zzzdaily.com/tracks/es/33-The_Fire_in_the_Cave.mp4">The Fire in the Cave</a> speaks to the moment when the darkness breaks and truth becomes visible. And the ZZZ Daily track <a href="https://zzzdaily.zzzdaily.com/tracks/es/41-Shedding_Proof.mp4">Shedding Proof</a> represents the evidence that can no longer be denied.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-survey-data">The Survey Data</h2>
<h3 id="what-the-numbers-show">What the Numbers Show</h3>
<p>Recent survey data reveals a dramatic shift in public perception of COVID-19 mRNA vaccines:</p>
<p><strong>Belief That Vaccines Have Caused Deaths:</strong> A significant and growing percentage of the public now believes that COVID-19 vaccines have caused a significant number of deaths. This represents a seismic shift from 2021, when the vast majority of the public accepted the "safe and effective" narrative without question.</p>
<p><strong>Personal Knowledge of Injury:</strong> A substantial percentage of respondents report personally knowing someone who they believe was injured or killed by a COVID-19 vaccine. This is no longer an abstract statistical debate — it is personal. When people see adverse events in their own families, workplaces, and communities, institutional denials become unpersuasive.</p>
<p><strong>Regret Among the Vaccinated:</strong> A meaningful percentage of vaccinated individuals now report regretting their decision to get vaccinated. This regret is strongest among those who received boosters and among those who experienced adverse events or witnessed them in people they know.</p>
<p><strong>Declining Trust in Public Health Institutions:</strong> Trust in the CDC, FDA, and NIH has fallen to historic lows. The decline is not limited to one political faction — it crosses demographic, political, and socioeconomic lines. The institutional credibility that was spent to promote COVID-19 vaccines has not been replenished.</p>
<p><strong>Increased Vaccine Refusal Across All Categories:</strong> The skepticism generated by COVID-19 vaccines is extending to all vaccines — including childhood immunizations. Parents who accepted the childhood vaccine schedule without question before 2020 are now questioning every injection. The pharmaceutical industry's reckless deployment of mRNA technology has undermined confidence in the entire vaccine enterprise.</p>
<h3 id="demographic-patterns">Demographic Patterns</h3>
<p>The awakening is not uniform across demographics, but it is broad:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Young adults (18-34):</strong> High rates of skepticism, driven by personal experience with adverse events (particularly myocarditis in young males) and social media information sharing</li>
<li><strong>Parents:</strong> Among the most rapidly shifting demographics, driven by protective instinct and personal observation of adverse events in children</li>
<li><strong>Healthcare workers:</strong> Paradoxically, frontline healthcare workers show some of the highest rates of skepticism — they witnessed the adverse events firsthand and many were injured themselves by mandated injections</li>
<li><strong>African American communities:</strong> High and rising skepticism, rooted in historical medical abuse (Tuskegee, etc.) and reinforced by COVID-era experiences</li>
<li><strong>Rural communities:</strong> High baseline skepticism amplified by COVID mandates and institutional overreach</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-kingston-report-what-drove-the-awakening">The Kingston Report: What Drove the Awakening</h2>
<p>Karen Kingston's analysis through <a href="https://karenkingston.substack.com">The Kingston Report</a> identifies the factors that drove the mass awakening.</p>
<h3 id="the-evidence-became-undeniable">The Evidence Became Undeniable</h3>
<p>Kingston has documented the cascade of evidence that broke through institutional suppression:</p>
<p><strong>Pfizer documents released.</strong> The court-ordered release of Pfizer's internal documents revealed adverse event data, manufacturing problems, and effectiveness failures that contradicted the public narrative. These documents were shared millions of times on social media, creating a decentralized information campaign that the institutional media could not suppress.</p>
<p><strong>VAERS data analysis.</strong> Independent analysts who downloaded and analyzed VAERS data found signals for death, myocarditis, stroke, blood clots, and dozens of other serious adverse events. While the CDC dismissed these analyses, the data itself was publicly accessible, and the analyses were shared widely.</p>
<p><strong>Excess mortality data.</strong> Insurance company actuarial data, national statistics offices, and independent analysts documented unprecedented excess mortality among working-age adults beginning in 2021 — temporally correlated with the vaccination campaign. The excess deaths could not be attributed to COVID-19 alone and were consistent with vaccine-mediated harm.</p>
<p><strong>Athlete collapses.</strong> A visible increase in sudden cardiac events among professional and amateur athletes — collapsing on the field, dying during competition — created a visceral, undeniable signal that something had changed in the health of young, fit people. The temporal correlation with mass vaccination was impossible to ignore.</p>
<p><strong>Personal testimonies.</strong> The most powerful driver of the awakening was not data but testimony. When individuals shared their personal experiences with vaccine injury — through social media, in community gatherings, in church groups — the aggregate impact was transformative. Each testimony represented a breach in the institutional narrative. Millions of breaches became a flood.</p>
<h3 id="the-institutional-response-failed">The Institutional Response Failed</h3>
<p>Kingston has documented that the institutional response to rising skepticism — more censorship, more mandates, more aggressive messaging — backfired catastrophically:</p>
<p><strong>Censorship amplified distrust.</strong> When social media platforms censored vaccine-critical content, it did not eliminate the content — it drove it underground and gave it the cachet of suppressed truth. People who might have accepted institutional messaging at face value began to question why truthful institutions would need censorship.</p>
<p><strong>Mandates generated resistance.</strong> Forcing people to choose between their jobs and an injection they distrusted did not create compliance — it created a permanent opposition. Every person fired for refusing a vaccine became an activist.</p>
<p><strong>"Safe and effective" became a mockery.</strong> As adverse events mounted and effectiveness waned, the mantra "safe and effective" became a symbol of institutional dishonesty rather than reassurance. The phrase is now used sarcastically across social media and popular culture.</p>

<h2 id="the-biblical-framework-the-remnant-awakens">The Biblical Framework: The Remnant Awakens</h2>
<h3 id="eyes-opening-across-the-earth">Eyes Opening Across the Earth</h3>
<p>The mass awakening parallels the biblical pattern of remnant consciousness — the moment when a significant minority within a population breaks free from the dominant deception and sees clearly:</p>
<blockquote>
<p>"And I will give them an heart to know me, that I am the LORD: and they shall be my people, and I will be their God: for they shall return unto me with their whole heart." — Jeremiah 24:7</p>
</blockquote>
<p>The awakening is not merely intellectual — it is spiritual. People are not just questioning vaccines. They are questioning the entire system that produced and mandated them — the pharmaceutical industry, the regulatory agencies, the media, the government, the medical establishment. This comprehensive questioning leads, inevitably, to deeper questions: Who has authority over my body? Who designed my body? What is the purpose of my body?</p>
<p>These questions lead to the Creator. The mass awakening to medical fraud is a doorway to spiritual awakening. When institutional trust collapses, the search for genuine authority begins — and genuine authority belongs only to God.</p>
<h3 id="the-remnant-pattern">The Remnant Pattern</h3>
<p>Throughout Scripture, the awakened are always a remnant — never the majority:</p>
<ul>
<li>Noah's family in a world of corruption</li>
<li>Lot in Sodom</li>
<li>The 7,000 in Israel who had not bowed to Baal</li>
<li>The faithful in Sardis who had "not defiled their garments"</li>
</ul>
<p>The survey data shows that the awakened are growing from a small minority toward a significant portion of the population. But even at 20-30%, the awakened remain a minority. The majority remains either compliant, uninformed, or in denial. The remnant pattern holds — the awakened see what the majority refuses to see.</p>

<h2 id="what-the-awakening-means-going-forward">What the Awakening Means Going Forward</h2>
<h3 id="for-the-pharmaceutical-industry">For the Pharmaceutical Industry</h3>
<p>The mass awakening represents an existential threat to the pharmaceutical industry's vaccine business model. If a significant portion of the population refuses future vaccines — including mRNA products for influenza, RSV, and other diseases — the revenue projections that justify billions in R&#x26;D investment collapse.</p>
<h3 id="for-public-health-institutions">For Public Health Institutions</h3>
<p>The CDC, FDA, and WHO face a credibility crisis that will take a generation to repair — if it can be repaired at all. Institutions that sacrificed their credibility to promote a single product cannot rebuild that credibility through messaging campaigns. Rebuilding requires structural change: accountability, transparency, and the restoration of genuine independence from pharmaceutical industry influence.</p>
<h3 id="for-individuals">For Individuals</h3>
<p>The awakening brings responsibility. Those who have seen the truth cannot unsee it. The awakened have a responsibility to share what they know — with family, friends, community, and especially with those who are still making medical decisions based on the institutional narrative.</p>
