15:T3264,<h1 id="will-faucis-covid-19-emails-finally-be-disclosed">Will Fauci's COVID-19 Emails Finally Be Disclosed</h1>
<h2 id="the-ancient-pattern">The Ancient Pattern</h2>
<blockquote>
<p>"For there is nothing covered, that shall not be revealed; and hid, that shall not be known. Therefore whatsoever ye have spoken in darkness shall be heard in the light; and that which ye have spoken in the ear in closets shall be proclaimed upon the housetops." — Luke 12:2-3</p>
</blockquote>
<p>When Daniel was thrown into the lion's den, the stone was sealed and the king's signet was placed upon it — to ensure that nothing concerning Daniel's fate could be changed. The adversaries who engineered Daniel's imprisonment believed the seal was permanent. But God opened what man had sealed, and Daniel emerged alive while his accusers were consumed.</p>
<p>As the Confliction track <a href="https://confliction.zzzdaily.com/tracks/es/31-Satans_Hosts.mp4">Satan's Hosts</a> reveals, the adversary's plans depend on secrecy. Exposure is their undoing. The communications sealed within Anthony Fauci's email archives represent the sealed stone — the record of decisions, coordination, and concealment that, if opened, could expose the full architecture of the pandemic response.</p>
<p>The question is whether the stone will be opened — and whether the nation has the courage to face what lies beneath it.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="what-the-emails-already-revealed">What the Emails Already Revealed</h2>
<p>The first tranche of Fauci's emails, released through FOIA requests in June 2021, revealed communications that contradicted his public statements on multiple critical issues:</p>
<p><strong>Lab leak origins:</strong> In January and February 2020, Fauci received private communications from prominent virologists — including Kristian Andersen of Scripps Research and Robert Garry of Tulane University — expressing concern that SARS-CoV-2 appeared to have features consistent with laboratory engineering. Within days, these same scientists authored a paper published in <em>Nature Medicine</em> ("The Proximal Origin of SARS-CoV-2") dismissing the lab leak hypothesis. The emails suggest that the reversal was coordinated rather than independent.</p>
<p><strong>Gain-of-function funding:</strong> Emails revealed discussions about NIH funding of research at the Wuhan Institute of Virology and the potential implications if the pandemic originated from NIH-funded research. Fauci's subsequent congressional testimony denying NIH funding of gain-of-function research at the WIV contradicted the substance of these internal communications.</p>
<p><strong>Masking:</strong> Early emails showed Fauci privately acknowledging that commercial masks provided minimal protection against respiratory viruses — a position he publicly reversed within weeks as masking became the administration's central public health recommendation.</p>
<p><strong>Early treatment:</strong> Communications revealed awareness of potential early treatment options that were subsequently suppressed in public guidance.</p>
<p>Karen Kingston has documented in The Kingston Report (karenkingston.substack.com): "The Fauci emails already released are damning. But they are a fraction of the total communications. The emails that remain undisclosed — particularly those from the critical January-March 2020 period when the pandemic response was being designed — are the ones that could establish whether the response was organic or orchestrated, whether the censorship was spontaneous or coordinated, and whether the suppression of early treatment was medical judgment or market protection."</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="what-remains-undisclosed">What Remains Undisclosed</h2>
<p>The emails already released represent a small portion of Fauci's total communications during the pandemic period. Significant categories of undisclosed communications include:</p>
<p><strong>Communications with pharmaceutical companies</strong> regarding vaccine development timelines, clinical trial design, and regulatory strategy.</p>
<p><strong>Communications with WHO officials</strong> regarding the pandemic declaration, travel restrictions, and international response coordination.</p>
<p><strong>Communications with social media companies</strong> or intermediaries regarding content moderation, censorship targets, and the definition of "misinformation."</p>
<p><strong>Communications with intelligence community officials</strong> regarding the lab leak investigation, the origin of SARS-CoV-2, and the coordination of the public narrative about pandemic origins.</p>
<p><strong>Communications with EcoHealth Alliance</strong> regarding gain-of-function research, Wuhan Institute of Virology funding, and the response to allegations of NIH-funded research contributing to the pandemic.</p>
<p><strong>Personal email accounts:</strong> Reports indicate that Fauci and other senior NIH officials may have used personal email accounts for official business — communications that would not be captured by standard government records retention and FOIA processes.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-disclosure-pressure">The Disclosure Pressure</h2>
<p>The push for full disclosure comes from multiple directions:</p>
<p><strong>Congressional investigations:</strong> House committees have issued subpoenas for Fauci's complete communications and have referred contempt proceedings against officials who have obstructed document production.</p>
<p><strong>FOIA litigation:</strong> Multiple organizations continue to litigate for the release of emails that federal agencies have withheld under deliberative process, law enforcement, and national security exemptions.</p>
<p><strong>Whistleblower testimony:</strong> Former NIH and CDC employees have provided testimony suggesting that communications were deliberately destroyed, misdirected to personal accounts, or classified to prevent disclosure.</p>
<p><strong>Public demand:</strong> Polling consistently shows that the majority of Americans believe Fauci's complete communications should be publicly disclosed.</p>
<p>The track <a href="https://zzzdaily.zzzdaily.com/tracks/es/181-Ending_Anonymity.mp4">Ending Anonymity</a> documents the irony of a surveillance state that monitors every citizen's communications while shielding its own officials' communications from public scrutiny. The same government that accessed Americans' phone records, email metadata, and social media activity through NSA programs claims the right to withhold its own officials' work communications from the public whose tax dollars fund their salaries.</p>

<h2 id="the-prophetic-dimension">The Prophetic Dimension</h2>
<blockquote>
<p>"And this is the condemnation, that light is come into the world, and men loved darkness rather than light, because their deeds were evil." — John 3:19</p>
</blockquote>
<p>The resistance to disclosure is not procedural — it is spiritual. The deeds documented in those emails were conceived in darkness and depend on darkness for their continuation. Every FOIA exemption invoked, every document classification applied, every delay strategy employed serves a single purpose: to prevent the light from reaching the darkness.</p>
<p>But Scripture promises that nothing covered will remain unrevealed. The stone over Daniel's den was unsealed. The tomb of Christ was opened. The sealed documents of the pandemic will, in time, be opened — whether by congressional subpoena, judicial order, or the final judgment at which all hidden things are brought to light.</p>

<blockquote>
<p><em>"And the LORD said unto Samuel, Hearken unto the voice of the people in all that they say unto thee: for they have not rejected thee, but they have rejected me, that I should not reign over them."</em> — <a href="https://www.biblegateway.com/passage/?search=1%20Samuel%208&#x26;version=KJV">1 Samuel 8:7 (KJV)</a></p>
</blockquote>

When Israel demanded a king like the other nations, God warned them exactly what would happen — their sons taken for war, their daughters for service, their fields and vineyards seized, a tenth of everything confiscated. They chose human government over God's reign, and the tyranny followed exactly as promised. Nothing has changed. The modern political system is two wings of the same bird — left and right, Republican and Democrat — all serving the same hidden power structure. Voting for either side is choosing which wing flaps next while the bird flies in the same direction. The real power has never been on any ballot.</p>
