<h2 id="the-ancient-pattern">The Ancient Pattern</h2>
<blockquote>
<p>"Thou shalt not bear false witness against thy neighbour." — Exodus 20:16</p>
</blockquote>
<p>The ninth commandment prohibits false testimony. When Pfizer told regulators, healthcare providers, and the public that its COVID-19 product was a conventional mRNA vaccine — a product that would deliver instructions, produce protein, and degrade — it bore testimony about what was in the vial. If that testimony was false — if the product contained self-amplifying components that were not disclosed — then the ninth commandment was violated at civilizational scale. False witness was borne against billions of neighbors who trusted that testimony with their bodies.</p>
<p>The Hebrew word for witness, <em>ed</em>, carries the weight of covenant. A witness in Israel stood before God as well as before men. To bear false witness was to invoke the curse of the covenant upon oneself. When a pharmaceutical corporation bears false witness about the contents of an injection administered to billions, the covenantal consequences are proportional to the scope of the deception.</p>

<h2 id="the-documents-what-pfizer-filed">The Documents: What Pfizer Filed</h2>
<h3 id="the-court-ordered-release">The Court-Ordered Release</h3>
<p>In January 2022, a federal judge ordered the FDA to release the documents that Pfizer had submitted in support of its COVID-19 vaccine's Emergency Use Authorization — documents that the FDA and Pfizer initially sought to keep sealed for 75 years. The lawsuit, brought by Public Health and Medical Professionals for Transparency (PHMPT), resulted in the rolling release of hundreds of thousands of pages of internal Pfizer documents.</p>
<p>These documents contain information that Pfizer never voluntarily disclosed to the public — information about adverse events, manufacturing processes, and, critically, the molecular composition of the product.</p>
<h3 id="patent-filings-and-self-amplifying-language">Patent Filings and Self-Amplifying Language</h3>
<p>Karen Kingston's analysis through <a href="https://karenkingston.substack.com">The Kingston Report</a> has focused on Pfizer and BioNTech patent filings — documents filed with the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) and international patent authorities that describe the company's mRNA technology in precise technical language.</p>
<p>Kingston has identified patent language describing:</p>
<p><strong>Self-Replicating RNA Elements:</strong> Patent filings describe mRNA constructs capable of "self-replication" — language that is consistent with self-amplifying mRNA technology. The patents describe RNA constructs containing sequences that encode RNA-dependent RNA polymerase (RdRp) activity — the enzyme that copies RNA and is the defining feature of self-amplifying mRNA.</p>
<p><strong>Alphavirus-Derived Sequences:</strong> Patents describe the use of alphavirus-derived nonstructural protein sequences — the same components used in acknowledged sa-mRNA products like ARCT-154. These sequences form the replicase complex that enables the RNA to copy itself inside human cells.</p>
<p><strong>Modified Nucleoside Constructs with Replication Capacity:</strong> Patent language describes modified nucleoside RNA (the same N1-methylpseudouridine technology used in Comirnaty) combined with elements that enable RNA amplification — suggesting that the modified mRNA in the commercial product may have self-amplifying properties that were not disclosed.</p>
<p><strong>Lipid Nanoparticle Formulations Designed for Self-Amplifying Constructs:</strong> Patents describe LNP formulations specifically optimized for the delivery of self-amplifying RNA — formulations that are similar to or identical with the LNP formulations used in the commercial COVID-19 product.</p>
<h3 id="manufacturing-process-documents">Manufacturing Process Documents</h3>
<p>Documents related to Pfizer's manufacturing process reveal a detail that has generated significant concern: the transition from <strong>Process 1</strong> to <strong>Process 2</strong> during commercial production.</p>
<p><strong>Process 1</strong> was used for the clinical trials. It produced mRNA through in vitro transcription using a linear DNA template — a high-fidelity method that produces relatively pure mRNA.</p>
<p><strong>Process 2</strong> was used for commercial production — the product that was actually injected into billions of people. It used a bacterial plasmid amplification method — growing the DNA template in E. coli bacteria before using it for mRNA transcription. This method is faster and cheaper but introduces the risk of DNA contamination.</p>
<p>The DNA contamination discovered by Kevin McKernan — including SV40 promoter sequences — came from Process 2. The commercial product was different from the clinical trial product. The public received a product that was never directly tested in the clinical trials as manufactured.</p>
<p>Kingston has documented that the Process 2 manufacturing method, combined with the patent filings describing self-amplifying constructs, raises the possibility that the commercial product contained self-amplifying elements that were not present in the clinical trial product — or that were present but not disclosed.</p>

<h2 id="what-self-amplifying-components-would-mean">What Self-Amplifying Components Would Mean</h2>
<h3 id="if-the-covid-shots-contained-sa-mrna">If the COVID Shots Contained sa-mRNA</h3>
<p>If Pfizer's COVID-19 product contained self-amplifying mRNA components — even partial or modified versions of sa-mRNA technology — the implications are:</p>
<p><strong>Billions received self-replicating genetic constructs without consent.</strong> Informed consent requires disclosure of the product's composition and mechanism of action. If the product self-amplifies and this was not disclosed, every injection was administered without valid consent.</p>
<p><strong>The duration of spike protein production was longer than disclosed.</strong> Pfizer and the CDC told the public that the mRNA would degrade within days. If the product contained self-amplifying components, spike protein production could continue for weeks or months — explaining the documented persistence of spike protein in vaccinated individuals far beyond the expected timeframe.</p>
<p><strong>The dose experienced was higher than the dose injected.</strong> If the mRNA amplifies, the actual amount of genetic material and protein produced in the body is far greater than the 30 micrograms per dose that was disclosed. The body manufactured additional doses internally.</p>
<p><strong>Shedding potential was higher than acknowledged.</strong> Self-amplifying constructs produce more protein, which means more potential shedding of spike protein to unvaccinated contacts.</p>
<p><strong>All adverse events may have been amplified.</strong> Every documented adverse event — myocarditis, thrombosis, neurological damage, cancer, reproductive harm — would be amplified by self-replicating mRNA because the causative agent (spike protein) is produced in greater quantities for longer durations.</p>

<h2 id="the-historical-precedent-pfizers-record">The Historical Precedent: Pfizer's Record</h2>
<h3 id="the-23-billion-criminal-settlement">The $2.3 Billion Criminal Settlement</h3>
<p>In 2009, Pfizer paid $2.3 billion to settle criminal and civil charges — the largest healthcare fraud settlement in U.S. history at that time. The charges included:</p>
<ul>
<li>Fraudulent marketing of Bextra (a painkiller) for uses the FDA had specifically declined to approve</li>
<li>Paying kickbacks to healthcare professionals</li>
<li>Making false claims about the safety and efficacy of multiple products</li>
</ul>
<p>This was not an isolated incident. Pfizer has paid billions in additional settlements for:</p>
<ul>
<li>Fraudulent marketing of Lipitor, Zoloft, and other drugs</li>
<li>Suppression of safety data for Celebrex and Bextra</li>
<li>Environmental violations</li>
<li>Foreign bribery</li>
</ul>
<p>A corporation that has paid billions in fines for lying about its products is now asking the world to trust its testimony about what was in the COVID-19 vials. The ninth commandment weighs heavily.</p>
<h3 id="the-pattern-of-concealment">The Pattern of Concealment</h3>
<p>Pfizer's documented pattern involves:</p>
<ol>
<li>Making strong safety claims during product launch</li>
<li>Suppressing or minimizing adverse event data</li>
<li>Fighting disclosure of internal documents</li>
<li>Eventually being forced to disclose through litigation</li>
<li>Paying fines and continuing operations</li>
</ol>
<p>The attempt to seal the COVID-19 vaccine documents for 75 years fits this pattern exactly. If the documents contained only evidence of safety and efficacy, there would be no reason to hide them. The request for 75 years of secrecy implies that the documents contain information that would damage the product, the company, or both.</p>
