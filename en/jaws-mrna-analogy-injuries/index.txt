<p>"The wicked flee when no man pursueth: but the righteous are bold as a lion." — Proverbs 28:1</p>

<p>In Steven Spielberg's 1975 film <em>JAWS</em>, the mayor of Amity Island refuses to close the beaches despite a deadly shark attack because tourism revenue is at stake. He suppresses evidence, silences warnings, attacks the credibility of those raising alarms, and keeps the beaches open — resulting in more deaths. The parallel to the mRNA injection rollout is so precise that it transcends analogy and enters the realm of prophecy.</p>
<p>The Jesuits, secret societies, Satanists, and the Catholic Church have always understood that economic interest is the most reliable tool of suppression. The Society of Jesus amassed enormous wealth through its global operations — Napoleon recognized their aim as "absolute power" exercised through every means, including financial. When pharmaceutical companies suppress injury data to protect revenue streams, they follow the same calculus: money over lives, profit over people, power over truth. Satan orchestrates this through his financial networks, ensuring that the love of money leads souls to damnation while their bodies suffer from pharmaceutical harm.</p>
<p>Howard Pittman's testimony revealed that the demonic realm strategically exploits human self-interest. The principalities of Ephesians 6:12 do not need to force human compliance — they merely need to align their objectives with human greed. When a pharmaceutical company can earn billions by suppressing injury data, the demonic agenda and the corporate agenda converge without any visible spiritual coordination. Pittman warned that 95% of hell's inhabitants are there for serving self. The pharmaceutical executives who serve quarterly earnings while people die from their products are serving self in its most lethal form. The track <a href="https://confliction.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/59-The_Deceivers_Lies.mp4">The Deceivers Lies</a> captures the systematic deception that protects revenue at the cost of lives.</p>

<h2 id="the-jaws-parallel">The JAWS Parallel</h2>
<h3 id="the-mayor-of-amity-island-a-pharmaceutical-archetype">The Mayor of Amity Island: A Pharmaceutical Archetype</h3>
<p>In <em>JAWS</em>, Mayor Larry Vaughn embodies the archetype of the authority figure who sacrifices public safety for economic interest. His behavior follows a predictable pattern: deny the danger, attack the messenger, reframe the narrative, and keep the money flowing. This pattern maps precisely onto the pharmaceutical industry's response to mRNA injection injuries.</p>
<p><strong>Deny the danger</strong>: The mayor insists the first death was a boating accident, not a shark attack. Pharmaceutical companies and regulatory agencies insisted that injuries following mRNA injections were "coincidental," "unrelated," or "expected side effects" — anything but evidence of a dangerous product.</p>
<p><strong>Attack the messenger</strong>: The mayor marginalizes Chief Brody, the local law enforcement officer who wants to close the beaches. Doctors and researchers who reported adverse events were stripped of credentials, censored on social media, investigated by medical boards, and labeled "anti-vaxxers" regardless of their qualifications.</p>
<p><strong>Reframe the narrative</strong>: The mayor focuses on economic necessity — the town depends on summer tourism. Pharmaceutical advocates focused on pandemic necessity — the world depends on vaccination. In both cases, the economic argument is used to override safety concerns.</p>
<p><strong>Keep the money flowing</strong>: The mayor keeps the beaches open; tourists keep coming; the shark keeps killing. Pharmaceutical companies keep producing; mandates keep injecting; injuries keep accumulating.</p>
<h3 id="the-bodies-in-the-water">The Bodies in the Water</h3>
<p>In <em>JAWS</em>, the consequences of the cover-up are visible: bodies in the water, grieving families, a community terrorized. The mRNA cover-up produces the same consequences, but the bodies are harder to see. They die in hospitals, at home, in their sleep. The deaths are attributed to "sudden adult death syndrome," "unexpected cardiac events," "coincidental timing." The grieving families are told they are imagining the connection. The community is not terrorized but sedated — convinced by institutional authority that the deaths are normal.</p>
<p>Karen Kingston has meticulously documented the injuries and deaths associated with mRNA injections — the myocarditis, the blood clots, the neurological damage, the immune system dysfunction, the sudden deaths in previously healthy individuals. The VAERS database (Vaccine Adverse Event Reporting System) has recorded unprecedented numbers of adverse events. Funeral directors have reported unprecedented rates of unusual clotting. Insurance companies have reported unprecedented increases in all-cause mortality.</p>
<p>The data is the equivalent of bodies in the water. The cover-up is the mayor insisting the beaches are safe.</p>
<h2 id="the-mechanisms-of-suppression">The Mechanisms of Suppression</h2>
<h3 id="regulatory-capture-as-beach-patrol">Regulatory Capture as Beach Patrol</h3>
<p>In <em>JAWS</em>, the mayor controls the beach patrol — the very institution responsible for public safety serves the economic interest of keeping beaches open. In the pharmaceutical world, regulatory agencies function as the beach patrol. The FDA, CDC, and WHO are theoretically responsible for public safety but are financially and institutionally captured by the pharmaceutical industry they regulate.</p>
<p>FDA receives significant funding from pharmaceutical user fees. CDC Foundation receives donations from pharmaceutical companies. WHO receives substantial funding from the Gates Foundation, which has massive pharmaceutical investments. The regulators are financially dependent on the regulated — a structural conflict of interest that guarantees that safety concerns will be subordinated to industry interests.</p>
<p>This regulatory capture explains how mRNA injections received Emergency Use Authorization despite inadequate safety data, how adverse event signals were dismissed rather than investigated, how clinical trial data was sealed for decades rather than released, and how the products remained on the market despite injury signals that would have prompted recall for any conventional pharmaceutical.</p>
<h3 id="media-as-the-town-newspaper">Media as the Town Newspaper</h3>
<p>In <em>JAWS</em>, the town newspaper could have warned the public but chose not to — the same economic pressures that silenced the mayor silenced the press. In the mRNA cover-up, mainstream media served an identical function. Pharmaceutical companies are among the largest advertisers on television, in print, and online. Media outlets financially dependent on pharmaceutical advertising revenue have no incentive to investigate pharmaceutical harm and every incentive to suppress it.</p>
<p>The result is a media landscape where mRNA injuries are either invisible or dismissed. Investigative journalism that would have exposed the cover-up was not conducted — not because the evidence was unavailable but because investigating would threaten advertising revenue. The media, like the town newspaper in <em>JAWS</em>, chose money over truth and complicity over journalism.</p>
