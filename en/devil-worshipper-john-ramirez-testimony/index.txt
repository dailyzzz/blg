15:T4d3d,<h1 id="from-devil-worshipper-to-deliverance-the-john-ramirez-testimony">From Devil Worshipper to Deliverance: The John Ramirez Testimony</h1>
<h2 id="the-ancient-pattern">The Ancient Pattern</h2>
<blockquote>
<p><em>"To open their eyes, and to turn them from darkness to light, and from the power of Satan unto God, that they may receive forgiveness of sins, and inheritance among them which are sanctified by faith that is in me."</em> — Acts 26:18</p>
</blockquote>
<p>The apostle Paul received this commission directly from the risen Christ on the road to Damascus. The language is precise. It does not say "to educate them" or "to reform them" or "to invite them to church." It says to OPEN THEIR EYES. To TURN them. From DARKNESS to LIGHT. From the POWER OF SATAN unto GOD. This is not metaphor. This is not religious poetry. This is the description of a transfer of allegiance — from one kingdom to another, from one master to another, from one spiritual reality to another.</p>
<p>John Ramirez lived on the dark side of that equation for over twenty-five years. He did not dabble in the occult. He did not experiment with Ouija boards at a slumber party. He was raised, trained, and commissioned as a high-ranking servant of Satan — a devil worshipper with a direct contract, operating under the authority of principality demons, executing assignments against the church, against families, against individuals who represented Jesus Christ. His testimony is not a cautionary tale about curiosity gone wrong. It is a firsthand intelligence report from behind enemy lines.</p>
<p>Howard Pittman saw the demonic hierarchy from above — five tiers of demons organized in military precision, principalities at the top, ordinary demons at the bottom. John Ramirez operated within that hierarchy from below. He answered to principalities. He carried out their orders. He was one of the weapons in their arsenal. When these two testimonies are laid side by side, the picture that emerges is terrifying in its consistency: the spiritual war is real, it is organized, and the church is losing.</p>

<h2 id="born-into-darkness-the-south-bronx">Born Into Darkness: The South Bronx</h2>
<h3 id="a-family-of-witches-and-warlocks">A Family of Witches and Warlocks</h3>
<p>John Ramirez did not choose the occult. The occult chose him. He was born into it — literally. He grew up in the South Bronx, one of the most dangerous neighborhoods in America. Poverty, violence, drugs, and despair were the fabric of daily life. But beneath the visible decay of the streets, something far darker was at work.</p>
<p>His ENTIRE father's side of the family consisted of witches and warlocks. This was not a casual interest. This was not folk religion or cultural superstition. They had what Ramirez describes as "a direct contract with the devil himself." A generational covenant. A bloodline agreement. The family did not merely believe in the devil — they served him, worshipped him, and received power from him in exchange for their allegiance.</p>
<p>This is a critical point that most Christians fail to understand. The devil does not operate on belief alone. He operates on contracts. On covenants. On binding agreements sealed in blood. When Ramirez says his family had a "direct contract with the devil," he is describing a spiritual transaction — authority and power given in exchange for worship and obedience. The same transactional structure that God uses with His people, the devil counterfeits with his.</p>
<h3 id="watching-his-father-worship-the-devil">Watching His Father Worship the Devil</h3>
<p>At eight or nine years old, John Ramirez witnessed something that would mark him for the rest of his life. He watched his father worship the devil in a room of the house — from 7 PM to 5 AM. Ten hours of unbroken demonic devotion.</p>
<p>His father spoke in demonic tongues — not the tongues of the Holy Spirit described in Acts 2, but the counterfeit version, the language of fallen spirits. He pulled flowers. He lit candles. He poured water. Every action was ritualistic, deliberate, and saturated with demonic protocol. There was a specific order to the worship. Specific words. Specific gestures. The devil demands precision from his servants, just as God demanded precision in the tabernacle rituals of the Old Testament.</p>
<p>A child watching this would be marked in one of two directions — either repelled toward the light or drawn deeper into the darkness. For Ramirez, surrounded by a family that normalized this practice, immersed in a culture that provided no alternative, the path was set. He was being drawn in. He was being prepared. He was being groomed.</p>
<h3 id="training-under-master-witches">Training Under Master Witches</h3>
<p>The training began early and it was relentless. Ramirez was not learning from amateurs. The witches who trained him had been practicing for thirty, forty, fifty or more years. These were people who had spent decades perfecting their craft, deepening their relationship with demonic entities, and climbing the hierarchy of Satan's kingdom.</p>
<p>The training was structured. It had levels. You did not begin by speaking to the devil. You had to earn the right. First, you learned to communicate with lower-ranking spirits. Then you progressed to more powerful entities. Then to principality spirits — the rulers of the demonic hierarchy that Pittman described. Only after years of proven service and demonstrated loyalty could you speak directly to the devil himself.</p>
<p>This structure mirrors military training. A new recruit does not begin by meeting the general. He starts at the bottom, proves himself, advances through the ranks, and earns access to higher levels of authority. Satan's kingdom is organized. It is disciplined. It is hierarchical. And it takes the development of its soldiers far more seriously than most churches take the development of their believers.</p>
<h2 id="the-first-contract-a-voodoo-necklace">The First Contract: A Voodoo Necklace</h2>
<h3 id="age-ten--the-recruitment">Age Ten — The Recruitment</h3>
<p>At age ten, Ramirez found a voodoo necklace at school. This was not an accident. In the spiritual realm, there are no coincidences. That necklace was placed in his path by design — a demonic appointment orchestrated by the principalities that had been watching him since birth.</p>
<p>This necklace became his "first contract with the devil." The first personal covenant. The family covenant already existed through his bloodline, but this was his own agreement — his own step into the darkness, his own willingness to receive what the devil was offering.</p>
<p>A tarot card reader — a witch — saw him and declared: "This boy, we want him." The word "we" is significant. She was not speaking for herself. She was not speaking for a group of humans. She was speaking for the demonic entities she served. The principalities had identified John Ramirez as a high-value recruit. They wanted him. They had plans for him. And they moved their human agents into position to acquire him.</p>
<h3 id="five-principality-demons-assigned">Five Principality Demons Assigned</h3>
<p>Five of the worst principality demons were placed on Ramirez as "spiritual guides" and "guardian angels." This is the devil's counterfeit of the Holy Spirit's indwelling and the ministry of angels described in Hebrews 1:14. Where God sends the Holy Spirit to guide, comfort, and empower believers, Satan assigns principality demons to guide, empower, and control his servants.</p>
<p>These were not low-ranking spirits. These were principalities — the top tier of the demonic hierarchy. Howard Pittman described this tier as the commanding level, the generals of Satan's army. For five of them to be assigned to a single ten-year-old boy indicates how valuable Ramirez was to the kingdom of darkness, and how significant his eventual defection would be.</p>
<p>The demons functioned as constant companions. They informed him. They directed him. They gave him supernatural knowledge and ability. They were, in every functional sense, his "spiritual life" — the dark mirror of what the Holy Spirit is to a genuine believer. Where the Spirit leads into truth, these principalities led into deeper deception. Where the Spirit produces love, joy, and peace, these entities produced power, fear, and destruction.</p>
<h2 id="astral-projection-and-spiritual-warfare">Astral Projection and Spiritual Warfare</h2>
<h3 id="leaving-the-body-to-curse-neighborhoods">Leaving the Body to Curse Neighborhoods</h3>
<p>By the time Ramirez was thirteen to sixteen years old, he was astral projecting — leaving his physical body and operating in the spiritual realm. This is not science fiction. This is not imagination. This is a spiritual reality that the Bible acknowledges. Paul himself described being caught up to the third heaven, "whether in the body or out of the body, I do not know" (2 Corinthians 12:2-3). The capacity to operate outside the physical body is real. The devil simply counterfeits it for his own purposes.</p>
<p>During these astral projections, Ramirez was not sightseeing. He was on assignment. He cursed entire neighborhoods. He placed spirits of prostitution on communities. Spirits of drug addiction. Spirits of homosexuality. Spirits of murder. Spirits of suicide. Each assignment was specific. Each spirit had a specific function. And each deployment was designed to destroy the people and communities that God loved.</p>
<p>This is the reality behind what many people dismiss as "social problems." When an entire neighborhood descends into violence, addiction, and sexual chaos, secular observers blame economics, education, or systemic inequality. But Ramirez reveals the invisible layer beneath the visible — the demonic operatives who are actively placing spirits of destruction on communities, families, and individuals. The social problems are real. But the cause is spiritual.</p>
<h3 id="born-into-hospitals--placing-death-in-icu-rooms">Born Into Hospitals — Placing Death in ICU Rooms</h3>
<p>By fifteen or sixteen, Ramirez had advanced to an even darker assignment. He describes being "born into hospitals" — entering medical facilities in the spirit to place death on patients. Specifically, he targeted ICU rooms, where the most vulnerable patients lay hovering between life and death.</p>
<p>This testimony should shake every Christian who has ever prayed for a sick loved one. While you were praying for healing, a devil worshipper may have been in the spiritual realm placing death on your family member. While you were trusting God for a miracle, a servant of Satan was executing an assignment to ensure that miracle never came.</p>
<p>The question is not whether this happens. Ramirez confirms that it does. The question is whether your prayers have enough power to overcome it. And the answer, for most Christians, according to both Ramirez and Pittman, is devastating: they do not. Because most Christians do not pray with authority. They pray with hope. And hope, without the power of a genuine relationship with Christ, is not enough to overcome a principality demon on assignment.</p>
<h2 id="the-wall-of-fire-encountering-christians-who-were-real">The Wall of Fire: Encountering Christians Who Were Real</h2>
<h3 id="nicky-cruzs-youth-group">Nicky Cruz's Youth Group</h3>
<p>In the midst of his years of devil worship, Ramirez encountered something he could not explain and could not overcome. He came across a Christian youth group in a schoolyard — a group connected to the ministry of Nicky Cruz, the former gang leader whose conversion is documented in the book "The Cross and the Switchblade."</p>
<p>Ramirez attempted to attack them. He was a high-ranking servant of Satan, empowered by five principality demons, trained by master witches, experienced in spiritual warfare. These were just kids. Christian kids. They should have been easy targets.</p>
<p>But there was a "wall of fire" protecting them. Something pushed Ramirez back. He could not touch them. He could not penetrate their spiritual covering. The power that protected those young Christians was greater than the power of the five principalities that empowered Ramirez.</p>
<p>This is the critical distinction that runs through every testimony we have examined. There is a difference between Christians who have a REAL relationship with Jesus Christ and Christians who have a religious routine. The youth group was covered. They were protected. The wall of fire was real. Not every Christian has this covering. But these did. And even a high-ranking devil worshipper could not break through it.</p>
<h3 id="the-power-of-genuine-love">The Power of Genuine Love</h3>
<p>What ultimately broke through Ramirez's defenses was not a theological argument. It was not a tract or a sermon or a worship song. It was love — genuine, unconditional, supernatural love. The kind of love he had never experienced in his entire life. Not from his family of witches and warlocks. Not from the devil who was his "daddy." Not from the principality demons who were his "spiritual guides."</p>
<p>The love of Christ, flowing through genuine believers, reached a place in Ramirez that the devil had never been able to fill. For all the power the devil gave him — the astral projection, the ability to place spirits, the authority over demons — the devil never gave him love. The devil cannot give what he does not have. And love is the one currency of heaven that hell cannot counterfeit.</p>
<p>This is why 1 Corinthians 13 places love above every other spiritual gift. Prophecy will cease. Tongues will cease. Knowledge will pass away. But love never fails. It was love that breached the walls of a devil worshipper's fortress. It was love that turned the darkness to light. And it was love that fulfilled the commission of Acts 26:18 — opening his eyes and turning him from the power of Satan unto God.</p>
<h2 id="the-deliverance">The Deliverance</h2>
<h3 id="coming-to-christ">Coming to Christ</h3>
<p>John Ramirez's conversion was not a casual decision. It was a war. When a high-ranking servant of Satan defects to the kingdom of God, the spiritual consequences are massive. Principalities that had invested decades in his development were not going to release him quietly. The battle for his soul was fought on multiple fronts — in the spiritual realm and in the physical, in his mind and in his body, in his relationships and in his circumstances.</p>
<p>But the blood of Jesus Christ is sufficient. It is sufficient to break every contract. Every covenant. Every generational curse. Every demonic assignment. The blood that was shed on Calvary has authority over every principality, every power, every ruler of the darkness of this world, and every spiritual wickedness in high places (Ephesians 6:12). Ramirez discovered that the blood of Jesus did what decades of devil worship could not — it set him free.</p>
<p>His deliverance was complete. The five principality demons that had served as his "spiritual guides" were cast out. The contracts were broken. The generational covenant was annulled. The man who had been born into darkness was born again into light. The fulfillment of Acts 26:18 — from darkness to light, from the power of Satan unto God.</p>
<h3 id="the-intelligence-he-brought-with-him">The Intelligence He Brought With Him</h3>
<p>What makes Ramirez's testimony uniquely valuable is not merely the drama of his conversion but the intelligence he brought with him from the enemy's camp. He knows how the devil operates. He knows the hierarchy. He knows the assignments. He knows which Christians are vulnerable and which are protected. He knows why most churches are defenseless and why most believers are easy targets.</p>
<p>This intelligence is not theoretical. It is operational. It comes from twenty-five years of service in the enemy's ranks. When Ramirez says that most Christians cannot pray for even one hour while he prayed to the devil all night long, he is not offering a spiritual opinion. He is reporting a tactical assessment from the front lines of a war that most Christians do not even know they are fighting.</p>
<p>The church would do well to listen to this intelligence. Not to glorify the devil. Not to sensationalize the occult. But to understand the enemy, his methods, his organization, and his targets — so that the body of Christ can finally mount an effective defense and fulfill the commission that Jesus gave: to open eyes, to turn people from darkness to light, and to deliver them from the power of Satan unto God.</p>
