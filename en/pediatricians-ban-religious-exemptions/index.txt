<h3 id="the-coordinated-campaign">The Coordinated Campaign</h3>
<p>The campaign to eliminate religious exemptions is coordinated across multiple levels: the American Academy of Pediatrics (AAP) lobbies state legislatures, pharmaceutical-funded advocacy groups provide political support, media campaigns create public pressure, and social media censorship suppresses counter-arguments.
