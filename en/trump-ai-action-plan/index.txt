<h2 id="the-ai-action-plan-unveiled">The AI Action Plan Unveiled</h2>

<p>The AI action plan announced by the Trump administration represents one of the most sweeping governmental embraces of artificial intelligence in American history. <p>The plan specifically calls for streamlining regulatory approval for AI systems, investing billions in AI research and development, creating AI-ready government databases, and deploying AI across military, intelligence, healthcare, and financial systems.
