15:T33c9,<h1 id="the-us-transhumanist-party-and-political-posthumanism">The U.S. Transhumanist Party and Political Posthumanism</h1>
<h2 id="the-ancient-pattern">The Ancient Pattern</h2>
<blockquote>
<p>"How art thou fallen from heaven, O Lucifer, son of the morning! For thou hast said in thine heart, I will ascend into heaven, I will exalt my throne above the stars of God... I will be like the most High." -- Isaiah 14:12-14</p>
</blockquote>
<p>The desire to transcend human limitations and become like God is not a modern philosophy. It is the original sin. It is the fall of Lucifer. It is the temptation in Eden. And it is now a registered political party in the United States of America. The U.S. Transhumanist Party has brought the ambition of Lucifer into the American political system, advocating for the technological transformation of humanity into something beyond human -- something posthuman -- something that replaces God's design with engineered enhancement.</p>
<p>The political posthumanist movement is not hiding its agenda. It states openly what the intelligence agencies, pharmaceutical companies, and military research programs pursue covertly: the fundamental alteration of the human species through technology.</p>
<p>As <a href="https://confliction.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/73-The_Fallen_Angels.mp4">The Fallen Angels</a> from the Confliction album declares, the desire to ascend beyond one's appointed station is the defining characteristic of the rebellion. And as <a href="https://confliction.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/01-Cast_Out_of_Paradise.mp4">Cast Out of Paradise</a> reminds us, the consequences of grasping at divinity through forbidden means are exile, corruption, and death.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-us-transhumanist-party">The U.S. Transhumanist Party</h2>
<h3 id="platform-and-goals">Platform and Goals</h3>
<p>The U.S. Transhumanist Party, founded in 2014 by Zoltan Istvan, is a political party that explicitly advocates for:</p>
<p><strong>Radical Life Extension</strong></p>
<p>The elimination of death through technological means, including genetic modification, organ replacement, brain uploading, and nanotechnology-based cellular repair. The party views biological death as a problem to be solved rather than an ordained aspect of the human condition.</p>
<p><strong>Human Augmentation</strong></p>
<p>The enhancement of human physical and cognitive capabilities through:</p>
<ul>
<li>Neural implants for cognitive enhancement</li>
<li>Genetic modification for physical enhancement</li>
<li>Nanotechnology for cellular repair and enhancement</li>
<li>Cybernetic integration for sensory and physical augmentation</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Morphological Freedom</strong></p>
<p>The right to modify one's own body in any way desired, including modification to non-human forms. This includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Gender modification (which the party supports as a subset of broader morphological rights)</li>
<li>Species modification (the right to add non-human features)</li>
<li>Cybernetic modification (the right to replace biological components with technological ones)</li>
<li>Digital embodiment (the right to upload consciousness into digital systems)</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Technological Rights</strong></p>
<p>The establishment of legal frameworks that recognize:</p>
<ul>
<li>The right of AI systems to legal personhood</li>
<li>The right to cognitive enhancement without restriction</li>
<li>The right to genetic modification without regulatory barriers</li>
<li>The right to technological immortality</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="the-political-strategy">The Political Strategy</h3>
<p>The transhumanist political strategy operates on multiple levels:</p>
<p><strong>Electoral Politics</strong></p>
<p>Transhumanist candidates have run for president, governor, and local offices, using campaigns as platforms to promote transhumanist ideas. While electoral success has been limited, the campaigns generate media coverage that normalizes transhumanist concepts.</p>
<p><strong>Policy Advocacy</strong></p>
<p>The party advocates for specific policy changes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Increased government funding for longevity research</li>
<li>Deregulation of genetic modification and nanotechnology</li>
<li>Legal recognition of digital persons and AI rights</li>
<li>Universal access to augmentation technology</li>
<li>Elimination of regulations that restrict human experimentation</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Cultural Influence</strong></p>
<p>Transhumanist ideas are promoted through entertainment media, technology journalism, academic conferences, and Silicon Valley culture. The normalization of transhumanist concepts in popular culture -- through films, television, video games, and social media -- is the predictive programming that prepares the population to accept what the party advocates politically.</p>
<h2 id="the-spiritual-analysis">The Spiritual Analysis</h2>
<h3 id="the-luciferian-template">The Luciferian Template</h3>
<p>The transhumanist platform follows the Luciferian template described in Isaiah 14 with precision:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>"I will ascend into heaven"</strong> = life extension and immortality technology</li>
<li><strong>"I will exalt my throne above the stars of God"</strong> = cognitive enhancement beyond natural limits</li>
<li><strong>"I will sit upon the mount of the congregation"</strong> = political power to impose transhumanist policies</li>
<li><strong>"I will ascend above the heights of the clouds"</strong> = transcendence of physical limitations</li>
<li><strong>"I will be like the most High"</strong> = the explicit goal of transhumanism: to become godlike through technology</li>
</ul>
<p>The transhumanist party does not merely pursue political goals. It pursues the spiritual objective of the original rebellion: to replace God's design with self-determined evolution, to achieve divinity through technology rather than through relationship with the Creator.</p>
<h3 id="genesis-6-repeated">Genesis 6 Repeated</h3>
<p>The transhumanist agenda is a political formalization of the Genesis 6 corruption. In Genesis 6, the sons of God crossed the boundary between the supernatural and the natural, producing hybrid beings (Nephilim) that were an abomination to the Creator. Transhumanism crosses the boundary between the human and the technological, producing hybrid beings (cyborgs, enhanced humans, digital minds) that violate the image of God.</p>
<p>The political legitimization of this crossing through a registered political party represents the normalization of what God judged with a flood. The transhumanist party asks the public to vote for their own genetic, biological, and spiritual corruption -- to democratically approve the Genesis 6 agenda.</p>
<p>Ana Mihalcea, MD, PhD, has documented on her Substack (anamihalceamdphd.substack.com) how the nanotechnology now found in human blood is the covert implementation of the transhumanist agenda that the U.S. Transhumanist Party advocates openly. What the party seeks through political channels is already being achieved through pharmaceutical channels -- without the consent or knowledge of the population being transformed.</p>
<h3 id="the-dome-and-the-deception">The Dome and the Deception</h3>
<p>The transhumanist dream of space colonization, digital immortality, and evolutionary transcendence collides with the reality of the firmament -- the dome that God placed over the earth in Genesis 1:6-8. Under this dome, humanity exists in its appointed place. The fallen angels are imprisoned under this dome. And the transhumanist dream of escaping human limitations is, in spiritual terms, the dream of escaping the dome -- of transcending the boundaries God placed.</p>
<p>But the dome holds. The firmament endures. And the transhumanist who dreams of uploading consciousness to escape the body still lives under the dome, on the plate of the earth, within the boundaries God set at creation. No amount of nanotechnology, genetic modification, or digital upload can breach the firmament. The only way through the dome is the way God ordained: death and resurrection through Jesus Christ. Every other path is an illusion -- a deception promoted by fallen beings who are themselves imprisoned under the dome they cannot escape.</p>
