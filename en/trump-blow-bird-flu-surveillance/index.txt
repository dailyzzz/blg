15:T3334,<h1 id="trump-delivers-shocking-blow-to-bird-flu-surveillance-program">Trump Delivers Shocking Blow to Bird Flu Surveillance Program</h1>
<h2 id="the-ancient-pattern">The Ancient Pattern</h2>
<blockquote>
<p>"The wicked flee when no man pursueth: but the righteous are bold as a lion." — Proverbs 28:1</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Pharaoh's sorcerers could replicate some of God's miracles — they turned their staffs to serpents, they turned water to blood, they summoned frogs. But their power was imitative, not creative. They could manufacture the appearance of a threat, but they could not control the consequences. When the real plagues came — the plagues they could not replicate — they confessed, "This is the finger of God."</p>
<p>As the Confliction track <a href="https://confliction.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/59-The_Deceivers_Lies.mp4">The Deceivers' Lies</a> reveals, the modern sorcerers manufacture threats through surveillance systems that are designed not to detect genuine dangers but to generate the data that justifies the next emergency declaration. The bird flu surveillance program was such a system — built to find what it was designed to find, calibrated to produce the alarm that triggers the response, and funded by the same interests that profit from the response.</p>
<p>Trump has delivered a blow to this system. The sorcerers are alarmed — not because the threat is real, but because their ability to manufacture the appearance of a threat has been disrupted.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-bird-flu-narrative">The Bird Flu Narrative</h2>
<p>H5N1 avian influenza has been promoted as the next pandemic threat for over two decades. Since 2003, public health authorities, pharmaceutical companies, and media outlets have periodically elevated bird flu to crisis status — warning of potential human-to-human transmission, projecting catastrophic death tolls, and advocating for massive investment in vaccines and antivirals.</p>
<p>The bird flu narrative intensified in 2024-2025 with the detection of H5N1 in U.S. dairy cattle herds and a small number of human cases among farmworkers. The CDC classified the human risk as low but simultaneously activated surveillance programs, allocated funding for H5N1 vaccine development, and coordinated with the WHO on international monitoring.</p>
<p>Karen Kingston has documented in The Kingston Report (karenkingston.substack.com) that the bird flu surveillance program followed the same pattern established with COVID-19: "The surveillance infrastructure is not designed to protect the public. It is designed to generate the data that justifies the next Public Health Emergency of International Concern declaration. Once a PHEIC is declared, the entire apparatus activates — EUAs, PREP Act immunity, vaccine mandates, censorship coordination. The surveillance program is the trigger. Whoever controls the surveillance controls the trigger."</p>
<h3 id="the-pcr-problem">The PCR Problem</h3>
<p>A critical element of the bird flu surveillance program relied on PCR (polymerase chain reaction) testing — the same technology used during COVID-19 to detect viral genetic material. During COVID-19, the PCR test was deployed at cycle thresholds so high that it generated massive numbers of false positive results — detecting fragments of viral RNA that did not represent active infection or transmissibility.</p>
<p>The bird flu surveillance program utilized similar testing protocols, raising the same concerns about diagnostic accuracy and case inflation that characterized the COVID-19 testing regime.</p>
<p>Kingston has noted: "The PCR test is not a diagnostic tool — it is a manufacturing tool. Kary Mullis, who won the Nobel Prize for inventing PCR, stated explicitly that it should not be used to diagnose infectious disease. When you run PCR at high enough cycle thresholds, you can find anything in anyone. The surveillance program was calibrated to find bird flu — and calibrated tools find what they are calibrated to find."</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-surveillance-infrastructure">The Surveillance Infrastructure</h2>
<p>The bird flu surveillance program encompassed multiple components:</p>
<p><strong>Animal surveillance:</strong> Monitoring of poultry flocks, dairy herds, and wild bird populations for H5N1 presence.</p>
<p><strong>Wastewater surveillance:</strong> Monitoring of municipal wastewater for H5N1 genetic material — a technique developed during COVID-19 and expanded to cover additional pathogens.</p>
<p><strong>Human surveillance:</strong> Monitoring of agricultural workers, veterinarians, and others with animal exposure for H5N1 symptoms and infection.</p>
<p><strong>Genomic surveillance:</strong> Sequencing of H5N1 viral samples to detect mutations that might enhance human transmissibility.</p>
<p><strong>International coordination:</strong> Data sharing with the WHO and international partners through global surveillance networks.</p>
<p>Trump's disruption of this program included the termination of federal contracts with surveillance operators, the withdrawal of funding for wastewater monitoring programs, and the cessation of federal coordination with international surveillance networks.</p>
<p>The track <a href="https://zzzdaily.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/165-Event_201_Came_True.mp4">Event 201 Came True</a> documents how pandemic simulations establish the infrastructure that is then activated during real events. The bird flu surveillance program was building the same infrastructure — surveillance systems, testing protocols, response frameworks — that would be activated when the next emergency was declared.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-response">The Response</h2>
<p>Public health officials and pharmaceutical companies reacted with alarm, warning that the dismantling of the surveillance program leaves the nation blind to the next pandemic threat. The WHO expressed "deep concern" about the United States' withdrawal from global surveillance cooperation.</p>
<p>But the alarm reveals its own motivation. The surveillance program's primary beneficiaries were not the American public but the pharmaceutical companies positioned to supply the countermeasures that the surveillance system was designed to trigger.</p>
<p>Multiple pharmaceutical companies had already invested in H5N1 vaccine development, with products in various stages of clinical trials. Federal contracts for H5N1 vaccine stockpiling had been negotiated or were in negotiation. The economic pipeline from surveillance detection to emergency declaration to EUA authorization to vaccine deployment was already being constructed.</p>
<p>Trump's blow to the surveillance program disrupts this pipeline at its origin — severing the detection mechanism that generates the data that justifies the emergency that triggers the deployment that generates the revenue.</p>
