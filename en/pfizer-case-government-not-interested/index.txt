15:T3197,<h1 id="court-filing-in-pfizer-case-government-not-interested-in-justice">Court Filing in Pfizer Case: Government NOT Interested in Justice</h1>
<h2 id="the-ancient-pattern">The Ancient Pattern</h2>
<blockquote>
<p>"How long, ye simple ones, will ye love simplicity? and the scorners delight in their scorning, and fools hate knowledge?" — Proverbs 1:22</p>
</blockquote>
<p>When Pontius Pilate washed his hands before the crowd, he did not declare Jesus innocent. He declared himself uninterested in justice. He knew the charges were false, he knew the accused was innocent, and he possessed the authority to release Him. Instead, he washed his hands — a public demonstration that the most powerful Roman official in Judea chose political convenience over righteous judgment.</p>
<p>As the Confliction track <a href="https://confliction.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/59-The_Deceivers_Lies.mp4">The Deceivers' Lies</a> reveals, the most damaging lies are not those told by the accused but by the authorities who refuse to prosecute them. When the justice system itself declines to pursue justice, the system becomes an accomplice.</p>
<p>The federal government has filed a court document indicating it is not interested in intervening in the Pfizer whistleblower case. The government has washed its hands.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-brook-jackson-case">The Brook Jackson Case</h2>
<p>Brook Jackson, a former regional director at Ventavia Research Group — a clinical trial subcontractor for Pfizer's COVID-19 vaccine trial — filed a False Claims Act lawsuit against Pfizer in January 2021. The lawsuit alleges that Pfizer submitted false claims to the federal government in connection with its COVID-19 vaccine clinical trial.</p>
<p>Jackson's allegations include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Falsification of clinical trial data</li>
<li>Failure to follow proper adverse event reporting procedures</li>
<li>Unblinding of trial participants</li>
<li>Inadequate training of vaccine administrators</li>
<li>Failure to maintain proper cold chain storage</li>
<li>Retaliation against employees who reported data integrity concerns</li>
</ul>
<p>Under the False Claims Act, the federal government has the right to intervene in whistleblower cases — taking over the prosecution, committing federal resources, and significantly increasing the likelihood of a successful outcome. Government intervention is a strong signal that the government believes the claims have merit and is committed to pursuing accountability.</p>
<p>The government's decision not to intervene sends the opposite signal: regardless of the evidence, regardless of the allegations, the federal government does not wish to pursue this case.</p>
<p>Karen Kingston has analyzed the filing in The Kingston Report (karenkingston.substack.com): "The government's refusal to intervene in the Jackson case is not a neutral act. It is a deliberate choice to protect Pfizer from accountability. The False Claims Act exists specifically to hold government contractors accountable for fraud. The government's refusal to use it against the largest government contractor in pandemic history tells you everything about whose interests the government serves."</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-legal-implications">The Legal Implications</h2>
<p>The government's non-intervention does not dismiss the case — Jackson can continue to prosecute it as a private plaintiff. However, non-intervention dramatically reduces the case's chances of success:</p>
<p><strong>Resources:</strong> As a private plaintiff, Jackson must fund the litigation against Pfizer — a company with effectively unlimited legal resources — from her own resources and those of her legal team.</p>
<p><strong>Discovery:</strong> Without government involvement, the discovery process is limited to what the private plaintiff can compel through the court. Government intervention would have brought the full investigative power of the Department of Justice, including access to classified documents, grand jury authority, and inter-agency coordination.</p>
<p><strong>Credibility:</strong> Jury perception is influenced by government involvement. A case prosecuted by the Department of Justice carries the weight of the federal government's endorsement. A case prosecuted by a private whistleblower must overcome the implied message that the government found the claims unworthy of pursuit.</p>
<p><strong>Pfizer's defense:</strong> Pfizer's legal strategy has relied heavily on the argument that the government was fully aware of the clinical trial's conduct and was satisfied with its results — the "government knowledge" defense. The government's non-intervention arguably strengthens this defense by implying agreement with Pfizer's position.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-governments-conflict-of-interest">The Government's Conflict of Interest</h2>
<p>The government's decision not to intervene cannot be evaluated without acknowledging the government's own conflicts of interest:</p>
<p><strong>The government co-developed the vaccine.</strong> The NIH co-developed the spike protein technology used in the Moderna vaccine and holds patent interests in related technology. The government has a financial interest in the success of the mRNA platform.</p>
<p><strong>The government purchased the vaccines.</strong> Under Operation Warp Speed, the government committed over $18 billion to vaccine development and procurement. Acknowledging fraud in the product undermines the government's own procurement decisions.</p>
<p><strong>The government mandated the vaccines.</strong> Federal agencies, the military, and government-adjacent institutions mandated COVID-19 vaccination for millions of Americans. Acknowledging harm caused by the product exposes the government to massive liability for the consequences of its mandates.</p>
<p><strong>Government officials face personal exposure.</strong> If the clinical trial fraud is proven, government officials who knew about data irregularities and chose not to act face potential criminal liability for conspiracy, obstruction, and misprision of felony.</p>
<p>The government is not a neutral party evaluating the merits of a whistleblower's claim. It is a co-conspirator evaluating whether to prosecute its own partner.</p>
<p>The track <a href="https://zzzdaily.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/151-One_Nation_Under_Blackmail.mp4">One Nation Under Blackmail</a> documents how institutional compromises create networks of mutual protection — where every participant's exposure ensures that no participant will pursue accountability against any other.</p>

<h2 id="the-prophetic-warning">The Prophetic Warning</h2>
<blockquote>
<p>"If thou seest the oppression of the poor, and violent perverting of judgment and justice in a province, marvel not at the matter: for he that is higher than the highest regardeth; and there be higher than they." — Ecclesiastes 5:8</p>
</blockquote>
<p>Solomon warns that the perversion of justice in human courts should not surprise the faithful — because there is One higher than the highest human authority, and He regards all things.</p>
<p>The government's refusal to pursue justice against Pfizer is the "violent perverting of judgment" that Solomon describes. But the earthly court's failure does not preclude the heavenly court's judgment. The evidence exists. The injuries are documented. The whistleblower has spoken. And the God who sees all things has noted the government's decision to wash its hands.</p>
