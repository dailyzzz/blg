15:T5926,<h1 id="informed-consent-destroyed-how-they-stole-your-right-to-choose">Informed Consent Destroyed: How They Stole Your Right to Choose</h1>
<h2 id="the-ancient-pattern">The Ancient Pattern</h2>
<blockquote>
<p>"I call heaven and earth to record this day against you, that I have set before you life and death, blessing and cursing: therefore choose life, that both thou and thy seed may live." — Deuteronomy 30:19</p>
</blockquote>
<p>From the very beginning, God established the principle of choice. In Eden, He placed the tree of the knowledge of good and evil in the garden and gave Adam a clear command — but He did not remove the tree. He did not make obedience automatic. He gave Adam the information, stated the consequences, and allowed the choice. This is the divine pattern of informed consent: full disclosure of risks, clear communication of consequences, and the freedom to choose.</p>
<p>The modern medical establishment has inverted this pattern. During the COVID-19 era, the information was suppressed, the consequences were hidden, and the choice was coerced. The Emergency Use Authorization framework bypassed decades of safety testing. Employers, universities, and governments imposed mandates that made experimental medical products a condition of employment, education, and participation in society. Those who questioned the safety data were censored, deplatformed, and labeled as dangerous misinformation spreaders. The result was the most comprehensive destruction of medical informed consent in modern history.</p>
<p>As the Confliction album track <a href="https://confliction.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/20-The_Great_Exchange.mp4">The Great Exchange</a> reminds us, the enemy's strategy has always been to offer a counterfeit exchange — trading something precious for something worthless, trading freedom for a false sense of security, trading the truth for a lie. The destruction of informed consent was precisely such an exchange: humanity traded its most fundamental medical right for the illusion of safety, and received neither safety nor freedom in return.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-nuremberg-code-born-from-atrocity">The Nuremberg Code: Born from Atrocity</h2>
<p>The Nuremberg Code was not written by philosophers in comfortable offices. It was written in the shadow of the most horrific medical experiments in human history. Following the defeat of Nazi Germany in 1945, the Nuremberg Military Tribunals prosecuted twenty-three German physicians and scientists for conducting experiments on concentration camp prisoners without their consent. These experiments included exposing subjects to freezing temperatures, high-altitude pressure chambers, malaria, mustard gas, and surgical mutilation — all in the name of advancing medical science.</p>
<p>The Nuremberg Code, established in 1947, articulated ten principles governing the ethics of human experimentation. The first and most fundamental principle states: "The voluntary consent of the human subject is absolutely essential. This means that the person involved should have legal capacity to give consent; should be so situated as to be able to exercise free power of choice, without the intervention of any element of force, fraud, deceit, duress, overreaching, or other ulterior form of constraint or coercion; and should have sufficient knowledge and comprehension of the elements of the subject matter involved as to enable him to make an understanding and enlightened decision."</p>
<p>Every word of this principle was violated during the COVID-19 vaccination campaign. The injections were authorized under Emergency Use Authorization, meaning they had not completed standard clinical trials. The recipients were not fully informed of the risks because the risks were not yet fully known. The mandates imposed by employers, schools, and governments constituted precisely the "duress" and "coercion" that the Nuremberg Code was designed to prevent. And the systematic censorship of safety concerns by social media platforms, government agencies, and mainstream media denied the public the "sufficient knowledge and comprehension" required for an enlightened decision.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="emergency-use-authorization-the-legal-loophole">Emergency Use Authorization: The Legal Loophole</h2>
<p>The Emergency Use Authorization (EUA) is the legal mechanism by which the U.S. Food and Drug Administration can authorize the use of unapproved medical products during a declared public health emergency. Under Section 564 of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act, an EUA allows the distribution of products that have not completed the standard approval process — which typically requires years of clinical trials, long-term safety monitoring, and rigorous review of manufacturing processes.</p>
<p>The EUA framework was designed for genuine emergencies — scenarios in which the immediate threat is so severe that the risks of using an unapproved product are outweighed by the risks of inaction. It was never intended to serve as a permanent bypass of the regulatory process, nor as a mechanism for mandating experimental products on an entire population.</p>
<p>Yet this is precisely what occurred. The COVID-19 vaccines were authorized under EUA beginning in December 2020. At that point, the clinical trials were barely months old. Long-term safety data did not exist. The Phase III trial participants had been followed for a median of only two months. And the trials themselves were structured in ways that precluded rigorous safety assessment — Pfizer unblinded and offered the vaccine to the placebo group within weeks, effectively destroying the control group that would have been essential for long-term safety comparisons.</p>
<p>Under 21 U.S.C. Section 360bbb-3, EUA products carry a specific legal requirement: recipients must be informed "of the option to accept or refuse administration of the product." This requirement — the legal codification of informed consent for experimental products — was systematically ignored. Millions of Americans were told they would lose their jobs, their military careers, their educational enrollment, and their right to travel if they refused the EUA product. This was not the "option to accept or refuse" that the law required. This was coercion, dressed in the language of public health.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-mandate-machine-coercion-disguised-as-policy">The Mandate Machine: Coercion Disguised as Policy</h2>
<p>The coercion operated at every level of society simultaneously, creating an environment in which the theoretical right to refuse was rendered practically meaningless.</p>
<p>On September 9, 2021, President Biden announced that all employers with 100 or more employees would be required to ensure their workers were either vaccinated or tested weekly. This executive order, implemented through OSHA, threatened businesses with fines of up to $14,000 per violation. Although the Supreme Court eventually blocked the OSHA mandate in January 2022, the threat was effective: millions of Americans received the injections during the months the mandate was in force, not because they chose to but because they feared losing their livelihoods.</p>
<p>The Department of Defense mandated COVID-19 vaccination for all active-duty military personnel in August 2021. Service members who refused faced disciplinary action, loss of benefits, and separation from the military. Thousands of service members were discharged for refusing the injection — in many cases, service members with years of honorable service, including those who had been deployed to combat zones, were dismissed for declining an experimental medical product.</p>
<p>Universities imposed vaccination requirements as a condition of enrollment. Students who had spent years working toward degrees were told they could not register for classes, live in dormitories, or attend in-person instruction without proof of vaccination. Many universities denied religious and medical exemptions, or made the exemption process so burdensome that it functioned as a de facto denial.</p>
<p>Healthcare systems — the very institutions tasked with upholding medical ethics — mandated vaccination for their employees. Nurses, doctors, and technicians who had worked through the earliest and most dangerous months of the pandemic, when no vaccine existed, were fired for refusing the injection once it became available. The irony was suffocating: the same healthcare workers celebrated as heroes in 2020 were dismissed as dangers to public health in 2021.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="what-patients-were-not-told">What Patients Were Not Told</h2>
<p>Informed consent requires that the patient receive complete, accurate information about the product, including its known risks, its limitations, and the availability of alternatives. During the COVID-19 vaccination campaign, the information provided to patients was incomplete at best and deliberately misleading at worst.</p>
<p>Patients were not told that the mRNA technology used in the Pfizer and Moderna vaccines had never been approved for use in humans before. They were told the technology was "well-studied," but they were not told that every previous attempt to develop mRNA vaccines — including for SARS-CoV-1, MERS, and RSV — had failed during animal trials, often due to a phenomenon called antibody-dependent enhancement (ADE), in which vaccinated animals developed more severe disease upon exposure to the target virus.</p>
<p>Patients were not told that the lipid nanoparticle delivery system — the microscopic fat bubbles used to transport the mRNA into cells — had a biodistribution profile showing that they did not remain at the injection site. Pfizer's own biodistribution study, conducted in Japan and obtained through Freedom of Information requests, showed that the lipid nanoparticles concentrated in the liver, spleen, adrenal glands, and ovaries within 48 hours of injection. This data was available to regulators before the EUA was granted, but it was not communicated to the public.</p>
<p>Patients were not told about the existence of the Pfizer Post-Marketing Experience report, which was submitted to the FDA in February 2021 and subsequently released under court order. This document listed over 1,200 categories of adverse events reported during the first three months of the vaccine's deployment, including myocarditis, pericarditis, autoimmune disorders, neurological conditions, blood clotting disorders, and death.</p>
<p>Patients were told the vaccines were "safe and effective" — a phrase repeated so relentlessly by government officials, media outlets, and social media influencers that it became a mantra, a catechism, a creed to be recited rather than a claim to be evaluated. The phrase was not a summary of the evidence. It was a marketing slogan deployed to short-circuit the critical thinking that genuine informed consent requires.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-censorship-apparatus-silencing-dissent-to-prevent-consent">The Censorship Apparatus: Silencing Dissent to Prevent Consent</h2>
<p>Informed consent cannot exist in an environment of systematic censorship. If the information necessary for an informed decision is suppressed, then the consent obtained is not informed — it is manufactured.</p>
<p>During the COVID-19 era, the United States government worked directly with social media platforms to censor speech that questioned the safety or efficacy of the vaccines. This was not speculation; it was documented in the Missouri v. Biden case (subsequently renamed Murthy v. Missouri), in which federal courts found that government officials had engaged in a "vast censorship enterprise" involving direct communications with Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, and other platforms to suppress content that contradicted the official narrative.</p>
<p>Emails obtained through discovery in this case revealed that senior officials at the White House, CDC, CISA (Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency), and the Surgeon General's office routinely flagged specific posts for removal, demanded that platforms take action against specific users, and pressured platforms to adopt more aggressive content moderation policies targeting "vaccine misinformation."</p>
<p>The definition of "misinformation" was elastic and self-serving. Claims that the vaccines did not prevent transmission — later acknowledged as true by CDC Director Rochelle Walensky — were initially censored as misinformation. Claims that the vaccines could cause myocarditis — later confirmed by the CDC's own safety monitoring systems — were censored. Claims that natural immunity provided durable protection — later supported by over 150 studies — were censored. Claims that the virus may have originated in a laboratory — now considered a plausible hypothesis by the FBI and the Department of Energy — were censored.</p>
<p>The censorship did not merely suppress dissent. It destroyed the informational ecosystem that informed consent requires. When a patient cannot access accurate safety data, when physicians are threatened with loss of licensure for discussing risks, when scientists who raise concerns are professionally destroyed, the concept of informed consent becomes a fiction — a legal formality that masks a reality of systematic coercion.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-physicians-who-spoke-out--and-what-happened-to-them">The Physicians Who Spoke Out — And What Happened to Them</h2>
<p>The destruction of informed consent required the silencing of the medical professionals whose duty it was to ensure it. Physicians who raised concerns about vaccine safety, questioned the mandate framework, or advocated for early treatment alternatives faced coordinated professional retaliation.</p>
<p>Dr. Peter McCullough, one of the most published cardiologists in history, was stripped of his academic positions and medical board certifications after publicly discussing COVID-19 treatment protocols and vaccine safety concerns. Dr. Robert Malone, who contributed foundational research to mRNA vaccine technology, was banned from Twitter and systematically smeared for raising concerns about the mass deployment of the technology he helped develop. Dr. Pierre Kory, a critical care physician who advocated for repurposed medications as early treatment options, was censored, investigated, and denied publication of his research.</p>
<p>The message to the broader medical community was unmistakable: dissent will be punished. This had a chilling effect on the practice of informed consent at the clinical level. Physicians who privately harbored concerns about the vaccines were reluctant to share those concerns with patients for fear of professional consequences. The medical profession, which is ethically obligated to prioritize the patient's right to information, instead prioritized institutional compliance — and patients paid the price.</p>
<p>As documented in the <a href="/pfizer-fraud-criminal-case/?lang=en">Pfizer Criminal Case</a>, the pharmaceutical company behind the most widely administered COVID-19 vaccine had already been convicted of criminal fraud and paid the largest criminal fine in corporate history. This information — directly relevant to a patient's assessment of the trustworthiness of the product — was virtually absent from the informed consent process.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-children-consent-by-proxy-coercion-by-design">The Children: Consent by Proxy, Coercion by Design</h2>
<p>The most ethically indefensible aspect of the informed consent destruction was the extension of vaccination mandates to children. Children cannot provide informed consent; they depend on their parents to make medical decisions on their behalf. Yet the system was designed to circumvent parental authority at every turn.</p>
<p>Schools required vaccination for enrollment, pressuring parents to comply or face the exclusion of their children from education. Vaccination campaigns were conducted at schools, in some cases without adequate parental notification. Adolescents were told they could consent to vaccination without parental involvement — a legal position adopted by several jurisdictions that effectively removed parents from the decision-making process.</p>
<p>The clinical trial data for children was extraordinarily thin. Pfizer's pediatric trial enrolled only 2,268 children ages 5-11, with a median follow-up of 2.3 months. This sample size was grossly inadequate to detect rare but serious adverse events. The trial was not powered to assess efficacy against severe disease or hospitalization in children — because severe disease and hospitalization in healthy children were so rare that they could not be meaningfully studied in a trial of that size.</p>
<p>The risk-benefit calculus for children was fundamentally different from that of elderly or immunocompromised adults. Children faced an extraordinarily low risk of severe COVID-19 — the infection fatality rate for children was estimated at less than 0.003%. The potential benefits of vaccination were therefore minimal, while the potential risks — including myocarditis, which disproportionately affected young males — were real and serious.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-historical-precedent-medical-ethics-after-nuremberg">The Historical Precedent: Medical Ethics After Nuremberg</h2>
<p>The Nuremberg Code was not the only ethical framework violated during the COVID-19 era. The Declaration of Helsinki, adopted by the World Medical Association in 1964 and revised multiple times since, established that "in medical research involving human subjects, the well-being of the individual research subject must take precedence over all other interests." The Belmont Report (1979), which governs human subjects research in the United States, identified three fundamental principles: respect for persons, beneficence, and justice — all of which require informed consent as a non-negotiable prerequisite.</p>
<p>The Common Rule (45 CFR 46), which codifies federal regulations for the protection of human subjects, requires that informed consent include: a statement that the study involves research; an explanation of the purposes and procedures; a description of foreseeable risks; a description of benefits; a disclosure of alternatives; and a statement that participation is voluntary and may be discontinued at any time without penalty.</p>
<p>Every one of these requirements was systematically undermined during the COVID-19 vaccination campaign. The products were experimental by definition — authorized under EUA, not approved through standard regulatory processes — yet they were presented to the public not as experimental products requiring careful informed consent but as established medical interventions requiring universal compliance.</p>
