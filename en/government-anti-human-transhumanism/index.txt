15:T46bb,<h1 id="the-us-governments-role-in-leading-the-anti-human-transhumanism-agenda">The U.S. Government's Role in Leading the Anti-Human Transhumanism Agenda</h1>
<h2 id="the-ancient-pattern">The Ancient Pattern</h2>
<blockquote>
<p>"And the serpent said unto the woman, Ye shall not surely die: For God doth know that in the day ye eat thereof, then your eyes shall be opened, and ye shall be as gods, knowing good and evil." — Genesis 3:4-5</p>
</blockquote>
<p>The promise of the serpent has never changed. It has only been repackaged. In Eden, the lie was wrapped in fruit. In our time, it is wrapped in executive orders, biotechnology initiatives, and public health mandates. The U.S. government — through funding directives, policy frameworks, and interagency coordination — has positioned itself as the world's leading institutional sponsor of a movement that seeks to fundamentally alter what it means to be human. This is not a conspiracy theory. It is published policy.</p>
<p>The Confliction track <a href="https://confliction.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/09-Satans_Disguise.mp4">Satans Disguise</a> captures the essence of this deception: the enemy does not announce himself. He comes clothed in the language of progress, health, innovation, and national security. And the U.S. government has become his most effective instrument.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="executive-order-14081-the-biotechnology-directive">Executive Order 14081: The Biotechnology Directive</h2>
<p>On September 12, 2022, President Biden signed Executive Order 14081, titled "Advancing Biotechnology and Biomanufacturing Innovation for a Sustainable, Safe, and Secure American Bioeconomy." This document, largely ignored by mainstream media, is one of the most consequential policy directives of the 21st century.</p>
<h3 id="what-it-says">What It Says</h3>
<p>The executive order directs all federal agencies to coordinate the development and deployment of biotechnology across every sector of American life. Its stated goals include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Advancing biotechnology and biomanufacturing to "further societal goals"</li>
<li>Using biological systems to produce "food and agriculture innovation"</li>
<li>Expanding "biobased products" across industries</li>
<li>Developing "gene-editing technologies" and "synthetic biology"</li>
<li>Building a "bioeconomy" in which biological processes replace traditional manufacturing</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="what-it-means">What It Means</h3>
<p>Stripped of bureaucratic language, EO 14081 directs the U.S. government to replace natural biological systems — including those in the human body — with engineered alternatives. The order explicitly mentions gene editing, synthetic biology, and biomanufacturing as national priorities. It calls for the development of "genetic technologies" to "write circuitry for cells" and to "program biology."</p>
<p>This is the language of transhumanism, embedded in presidential policy. The government is not merely permitting the modification of living organisms — it is mandating it as a national strategic objective.</p>
<p>Karen Kingston's Substack (karenkingston.substack.com) published a detailed analysis of EO 14081, demonstrating how its language maps directly onto the transhumanist agenda: the redesign of human biology through biotechnology, gene editing, and synthetic systems.</p>
<h3 id="the-bioeconomy-framework">The Bioeconomy Framework</h3>
<p>The executive order establishes a "bioeconomy" as a new economic paradigm. In a bioeconomy, biological processes — including those occurring in the human body — are treated as economic inputs. Your cells, your DNA, your biological data are not private possessions but resources to be harvested, processed, and monetized.</p>
<p>This is not a theoretical concern. The bioeconomy framework means that human biology is being integrated into the supply chain. As the ZZZ Daily track <a href="https://zzzdaily.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/215-Brain_Machine_Interface.mp4">Brain Machine Interface</a> illustrates, the interface between human biology and technological systems is being built at every level — from the cellular to the cognitive.</p>
<h2 id="the-ostp-transhumanism-report">The OSTP Transhumanism Report</h2>
<h3 id="the-office-of-science-and-technology-policy">The Office of Science and Technology Policy</h3>
<p>The White House Office of Science and Technology Policy (OSTP) has published multiple reports advancing the biotechnology agenda. These reports frame human biological modification not as a distant possibility but as an immediate policy objective.</p>
<p>In coordination with the National Science Foundation, the National Institutes of Health, the Department of Defense, and the Department of Energy, OSTP has developed roadmaps for:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Whole-genome sequencing</strong> of the American population</li>
<li><strong>Gene therapy</strong> as standard medical practice</li>
<li><strong>Neurotechnology</strong> development for civilian and military applications</li>
<li><strong>Synthetic biology</strong> standardization and commercialization</li>
<li><strong>Biometric data</strong> collection and integration</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="the-language-of-enhancement">The Language of Enhancement</h3>
<p>Government documents consistently use the language of "enhancement" rather than "modification." This rhetorical choice is deliberate. Enhancement suggests improvement — making something better. But the transhumanist definition of "better" means "less human." A human being enhanced with synthetic biology, neural implants, and genetic modifications is, by definition, no longer fully human. The enhancement is the destruction.</p>
<p>The ZZZ Daily track <a href="https://zzzdaily.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/339-The_New_Man.mp4">The New Man</a> addresses this directly: the "new man" being engineered by government policy is not an improved human but a replacement human — a being designed to specifications rather than created in the image of God.</p>
<h2 id="darpa-and-the-military-pipeline">DARPA and the Military Pipeline</h2>
<h3 id="the-soldier-of-the-future">The Soldier of the Future</h3>
<p>As documented in our article on <a href="/darpa-synthetic-biology-budget/?lang=en">DARPA's $4.1 Billion Synthetic Biology Budget</a>, the Defense Department is the single largest funder of transhumanist technology. But DARPA's influence extends far beyond the military.</p>
<p>Every major technology platform in civilian use today — the internet, GPS, touch screens, voice recognition, autonomous vehicles — was first developed under DARPA funding. The same pipeline now operates for biotechnology: DARPA develops the capability, demonstrates it in military applications, and then transfers it to commercial partners for civilian deployment.</p>
<p>The technologies DARPA is developing today — brain-computer interfaces, gene editing, synthetic biology, neural modification — will follow the same trajectory. They are military technologies being prepared for civilian deployment.</p>
<h3 id="the-intelligence-communitys-interest">The Intelligence Community's Interest</h3>
<p>The CIA's venture capital arm, In-Q-Tel, has invested in numerous biotechnology and synthetic biology companies. The National Security Agency collects biometric data on a global scale. The intelligence community views human biology not as a sacred domain but as an intelligence resource — a source of data to be collected, analyzed, and leveraged.</p>
<p>The Confliction track <a href="https://confliction.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/67-The_Enemy_Within.mp4">The Enemy Within</a> speaks to this reality: the greatest threat is not an external army but a system that operates within — within the government, within the body, within the cells themselves.</p>
<h2 id="nih-and-the-genetic-agenda">NIH and the Genetic Agenda</h2>
<h3 id="the-human-genome-project-legacy">The Human Genome Project Legacy</h3>
<p>The National Institutes of Health led the Human Genome Project, which mapped the entire human genome by 2003. This achievement was celebrated as a triumph of science. But it also created the foundational tool for genetic modification: you cannot edit what you have not mapped.</p>
<p>Since the completion of the Human Genome Project, NIH funding for genetic research has accelerated dramatically. Programs include:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>The All of Us Research Program</strong> — collecting genetic data from one million Americans</li>
<li><strong>The BRAIN Initiative</strong> — mapping the human brain at cellular resolution</li>
<li><strong>The Somatic Cell Genome Editing Program</strong> — developing tools to edit genes in living patients</li>
<li><strong>The Common Fund's Human BioMolecular Atlas Program</strong> — mapping every cell in the human body</li>
</ul>
<p>Each of these programs provides data and tools for the transhumanist agenda. Mapping the genome enables editing it. Mapping the brain enables interfacing with it. Mapping every cell enables reprogramming them.</p>
<h3 id="nih-funding-of-gain-of-function-research">NIH Funding of Gain-of-Function Research</h3>
<p>NIH's funding of gain-of-function research — through EcoHealth Alliance and other intermediaries — is now well documented. The modification of natural pathogens to enhance their transmissibility and lethality is the dark side of the same genetic technologies being promoted for human "enhancement."</p>
<p>The same tools that edit genes to "cure disease" can edit genes to create disease. The same institutional infrastructure that promises to heal is capable of harming on a global scale. As Karen Kingston has documented on her Substack (karenkingston.substack.com), the line between public health and bioweapons has been deliberately erased by institutional policy.</p>
<h2 id="the-fdas-role-regulatory-capture">The FDA's Role: Regulatory Capture</h2>
<h3 id="approving-the-unapproved">Approving the Unapproved</h3>
<p>The Food and Drug Administration is tasked with protecting the American public from unsafe drugs and medical devices. Yet the FDA has systematically lowered its standards to accommodate biotechnology products, including:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Emergency Use Authorizations</strong> that bypass standard safety testing</li>
<li><strong>Accelerated approval pathways</strong> for gene therapies</li>
<li><strong>Regulatory frameworks</strong> that classify gene-editing technologies as "vaccines" rather than gene therapies</li>
<li><strong>Expedited reviews</strong> for synthetic biology products</li>
</ul>
<p>This regulatory capture means that transhumanist technologies enter the human body without the safety testing that would be required for a conventional drug. The FDA has become not a gatekeeper but a gateway — facilitating the deployment of technologies that alter human biology.</p>
<p>The ZZZ Daily track <a href="https://zzzdaily.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/43-Singularity_2030.mp4">Singularity 2030</a> projects the timeline: by 2030, the convergence of biotechnology, artificial intelligence, and nanotechnology is expected to produce systems capable of fundamentally altering the human species. The government's regulatory apparatus is being prepared to approve these technologies, not to prevent them.</p>
<h2 id="the-prophetic-context">The Prophetic Context</h2>
<h3 id="the-beast-system-infrastructure">The Beast System Infrastructure</h3>
<p>Revelation 13 describes a system of total control — economic, biological, and spiritual. The mark of the beast is placed "in" the right hand or forehead, controls participation in the economy, and is associated with the worship of the beast. This system requires:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Technology</strong> to modify the human body at a biological level</li>
<li><strong>Infrastructure</strong> to track and monitor every individual</li>
<li><strong>Legal authority</strong> to mandate participation</li>
<li><strong>Regulatory approval</strong> for the biological component</li>
</ol>
<p>The U.S. government is building all four components simultaneously. DARPA develops the technology. The NSA and intelligence community build the infrastructure. Executive orders establish the legal authority. The FDA provides regulatory approval. The beast system is not coming — it is being constructed in real time, by identifiable agencies, with published budgets.</p>
<h3 id="the-tower-of-babel-repeated">The Tower of Babel Repeated</h3>
<p>Genesis 11 records humanity's first collective technological project: the Tower of Babel. Its purpose was to "reach unto heaven" — to bridge the gap between human and divine. God's response was to confound their language and scatter them across the earth. The sin was not construction but aspiration: humanity sought to transcend its created limitations and achieve divine status through technology.</p>
<p>The transhumanism agenda is the Tower of Babel rebuilt. Its purpose is identical: to transcend human limitations through technology. To become "as gods." To overcome death, disease, and the boundaries of human biology through engineering rather than through God.</p>
<p>The ZZZ Daily track <a href="https://zzzdaily.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/495-Tower_Of_Babel_2.mp4">Tower Of Babel 2</a> makes this connection explicit: we are watching the second attempt to build a tower to heaven, and it is being funded by the U.S. government.</p>

<p>Believers must educate themselves and others. The technologies being developed are not neutral tools — they are components of a system that scripture warns will be used to enslave humanity. Understanding the specifics — the programs, the budgets, the timelines — is essential for informed resistance.</p>
