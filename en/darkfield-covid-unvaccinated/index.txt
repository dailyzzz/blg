<h3 id="methodology">Methodology</h3>
<p>The methodology is consistent across research groups:</p>
<ol>

<li>A fresh blood sample is drawn via finger prick or venipuncture</li>
<li>The sample is placed on a glass slide with coverslip</li>
<li>The slide is examined under darkfield microscopy at magnifications from 100x to 4000x</li>
<li>Observations are documented through photography and video</li>
<li>In many cases, time-lapse documentation captures real-time changes over hours</li>
