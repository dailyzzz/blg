<h2 id="the-ancient-pattern">The Ancient Pattern</h2>
<blockquote>
<p>"And they brought young children to him, that he should touch them: and his disciples rebuked those that brought them. But when Jesus saw it, he was much displeased, and said unto them, Suffer the little children to come unto me, and forbid them not: for of such is the kingdom of God." — Mark 10:13-14</p>
</blockquote>
<p>When children were brought to Jesus, the disciples tried to turn them away. They thought the Master's time was too important, His mission too grand, for the concerns of children. Jesus rebuked them. He declared that the kingdom of God belongs to children — and that anyone who prevents children from reaching Him faces His displeasure.</p>
<p>As the Confliction track <a href="https://confliction.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/07-The_Serpents_Curse.mp4">The Serpent's Curse</a> reveals, the serpent's curse targets the most vulnerable — and children are the most vulnerable of all. An epidemic of neurological injury among children is not a mystery that defies investigation. It is a crime that has been protected from investigation by the institutions that should have investigated it decades ago.</p>
<p>RFK Jr. has announced a worldwide effort to determine the cause of autism. For the first time in history, a federal official with the authority and the will is demanding an answer to the question the medical establishment has refused to ask.</p>

<h2 id="the-autism-epidemic">The Autism Epidemic</h2>
<p>In 1970, the prevalence of autism spectrum disorder (ASD) in the United States was estimated at approximately 1 in 10,000 children. By 2000, it had risen to 1 in 150. By 2010, 1 in 68. By 2020, 1 in 36. The most recent CDC estimates suggest the rate may now exceed 1 in 30 children.</p>
<p>This is not a gradual increase. It is an exponential explosion — a more than 300-fold increase in five decades. No genetic change can produce a 300-fold increase in prevalence in two generations. The cause is environmental — something in the environment of American children has changed dramatically since 1970, and that change is producing neurological injury at epidemic scale.</p>
<p>The medical establishment's response to this epidemic has been to deny its existence, attribute it to "better diagnosis," and refuse to investigate environmental causes. The CDC, which is responsible for monitoring autism prevalence, has simultaneously:</p>
<ul>
<li>Documented the increasing rates</li>
<li>Refused to conduct comparative studies of vaccinated vs. unvaccinated children</li>
<li>Funded research focused on genetic factors rather than environmental exposures</li>
<li>Actively suppressed research linking environmental exposures to autism</li>
<li>Characterized anyone who questions environmental causes as "anti-science"</li>
</ul>
<p>Karen Kingston has documented in The Kingston Report (karenkingston.substack.com): "The refusal to study the environmental causes of autism is the greatest scientific scandal in modern history. We have a 300-fold increase in a neurological condition affecting children. The CDC knows the rate is increasing. The CDC refuses to study why. The only logical explanation for this refusal is that the agency already knows — or suspects — that the answer implicates products it recommends, industries it protects, or policies it has implemented."</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-investigation">The Investigation</h2>
<p>RFK Jr.'s worldwide investigation will pursue multiple lines of inquiry simultaneously:</p>
<p><strong>Vaccinated vs. unvaccinated comparisons:</strong> The study the CDC has refused to conduct for decades — a comprehensive comparison of health outcomes, including autism rates, between fully vaccinated and completely unvaccinated children.</p>
<p><strong>Temporal correlation analysis:</strong> Detailed analysis of the correlation between the expansion of the childhood vaccination schedule, the introduction of specific vaccines, and the acceleration of autism prevalence.</p>
<p><strong>Environmental toxin assessment:</strong> Investigation of the cumulative effect of environmental exposures — including pesticides, heavy metals, food additives, endocrine disruptors, and pharmaceutical residues — on neurodevelopment.</p>
<p><strong>Aluminum adjuvant research:</strong> Study of the neurological effects of aluminum adjuvants used in childhood vaccines, which have been documented to accumulate in brain tissue.</p>
<p><strong>International comparisons:</strong> Comparison of autism rates across nations with different vaccination schedules, environmental regulations, and dietary patterns to identify correlating factors.</p>
<p><strong>Regression analysis:</strong> Investigation of "regressive autism" — cases in which children develop normally and then lose language, social, and cognitive abilities — including temporal proximity to environmental exposures.</p>
<p>The investigation will be conducted through international partnerships with research institutions independent of pharmaceutical industry funding — a critical distinction, as the majority of autism research conducted in the United States has been funded by institutions with pharmaceutical industry connections.</p>
<hr>
<h2 id="the-cdc-whistleblower">The CDC Whistleblower</h2>
<p>In 2014, Dr. William Thompson, a senior CDC scientist, disclosed to Congress that he and his colleagues at the CDC had committed research fraud in a 2004 study examining the relationship between the MMR vaccine and autism.</p>
<p>Thompson alleged that the study's authors — including himself — had identified a statistically significant association between the MMR vaccine and autism in African American boys. Rather than report this finding, Thompson alleged, the authors met and agreed to exclude the data that produced the association. Thompson claimed he was instructed to destroy documents related to the original analysis, though he retained copies.</p>
<p>Thompson's allegations were never investigated by the CDC. He was not called to testify before Congress despite offering to do so under oath. The original study remains in the literature, uncorrected. The data Thompson described has never been independently reviewed.</p>
<p>RFK Jr.'s investigation will include a review of the Thompson allegations and the original data — potentially providing the first independent analysis of the study that the CDC allegedly manipulated.</p>
