<h2 id="tamburini-i-govern-not-only-to-paris-but-to-china">Tamburini: "I Govern Not Only to Paris But to China"</h2>

<p>Michelangelo Tamburini served as the fourteenth Superior General of the Society of Jesus from 1706 to 1730. From his chamber in Rome, Tamburini directed Jesuit operations on every inhabited continent — from the courts of Europe to the missions of China, from the plantations of South America to the trading posts of India.</p>
<p>And Tamburini, in a moment of extraordinary candor, admitted the scope of his power:</p>
<p><em>"See sir, from this chamber I govern not only to Paris but to China, not only to China but to all the world, without anyone to know how I do it."</em></p>
