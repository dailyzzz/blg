<h2 id="the-ancient-pattern">The Ancient Pattern</h2>
<blockquote>
<p>"Put not your trust in princes, nor in the son of man, in whom there is no help." -- Psalm 146:3</p>
</blockquote>
<p>The psalmist's warning is not merely spiritual counsel -- it is practical wisdom drawn from millennia of observing the behavior of earthly rulers. Princes lie. Governments deceive. Institutions of authority consistently prioritize their own survival, their own funding, and their own agendas over the welfare of the people they claim to serve. This is not cynicism. It is documented history.</p>
<p>Every generation is told that the current biotechnology is safe. Every generation discovers, too late, that the assurances were false. From DDT to thalidomide, from Agent Orange to Vioxx, from SV40-contaminated polio vaccines to mRNA nanotechnology, the pattern is unbroken: government declares safety, population accepts, damage occurs, truth emerges decades later, and no one is held accountable.</p>
<p>As <a href="https://confliction.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/59-The_Deceivers_Lies.mp4">The Deceivers Lies</a> from the Confliction album declares, the father of lies operates through systems of institutional authority. And as <a href="https://confliction.zzzdaily.com/tracks/en/07-The_Serpents_Curse.mp4">The Serpents Curse</a> warns, the curse that enters through deception compounds through generations.</p>

<h2 id="the-historical-record">The Historical Record</h2>
<h3 id="ddt-safe-as-your-kitchen-table">DDT: "Safe As Your Kitchen Table"</h3>
<p>In the 1940s and 1950s, the United States government and the chemical industry aggressively promoted DDT as safe for human contact. Government-produced films showed children being directly sprayed with DDT. Advertisements featured smiling housewives applying DDT in kitchens. The USDA declared DDT safe for agricultural use on food crops.</p>
<p>By the 1960s, evidence of DDT's devastating effects was undeniable: cancer, reproductive failure, endocrine disruption, and ecological destruction. Rachel Carson's "Silent Spring" (1962) documented the damage. DDT was banned in the United States in 1972 -- nearly three decades after government agencies declared it safe.</p>
<p>The pattern: government declares safety. Population accepts. Damage occurs. Truth emerges decades later. No one is held accountable.</p>
<h3 id="thalidomide-safe-for-morning-sickness">Thalidomide: "Safe For Morning Sickness"</h3>
<p>In the late 1950s, thalidomide was prescribed to pregnant women for morning sickness, approved by regulatory agencies in multiple countries as safe. By 1961, thousands of children had been born with severe birth defects -- missing limbs, malformed organs, and neurological damage. Over 10,000 children were affected worldwide.</p>
<p>The FDA's refusal to approve thalidomide in the United States (thanks to the persistence of FDA reviewer Frances Kelsey) is held up as a success story. But the broader lesson is that regulatory agencies in multiple countries approved a product that caused devastating birth defects -- and would have continued approving it had the damage not been so visually undeniable.</p>
<h3 id="agent-orange-safe-for-military-use">Agent Orange: "Safe For Military Use"</h3>
<p>During the Vietnam War, the U.S. military sprayed approximately 20 million gallons of Agent Orange -- a herbicide containing the toxic contaminant dioxin -- over Vietnam. Government assurances that Agent Orange was safe for military personnel were unequivocal. Soldiers were told they could handle the chemical without protective equipment.</p>
<p>Decades later, Agent Orange has been linked to cancer, diabetes, birth defects, and neurological disorders in both American veterans and Vietnamese civilians. The VA now recognizes over 14 diseases as Agent Orange-related conditions. Millions of people were exposed based on government assurances of safety.</p>
<h3 id="vioxx-safe-for-pain-management">Vioxx: "Safe For Pain Management"</h3>
<p>In 1999, Merck's Vioxx (rofecoxib) was approved by the FDA as a safe anti-inflammatory medication. Internal Merck documents, later revealed in litigation, showed that the company knew of cardiovascular risks before approval but concealed the data. The FDA approved the product and maintained its safety classification for five years.</p>
<p>By the time Vioxx was withdrawn in 2004, an estimated 60,000 Americans had died from Vioxx-related heart attacks and strokes. The FDA's own safety reviewer, Dr. David Graham, testified before Congress that the FDA was "incapable of protecting America" against unsafe drugs.</p>
<h3 id="the-sv40-polio-vaccine-contamination">The SV40 Polio Vaccine Contamination</h3>
<p>As documented in our article on SV40, between 1955 and 1963, millions of Americans received polio vaccines contaminated with SV40 -- a cancer-causing monkey virus. The government knew about the contamination by 1960 but took no public action. No recall was issued. No notification was made. The contaminated vaccines continued to be administered for three additional years.</p>
<p>Government assurance of safety: absolute. Actual safety: cancer-causing virus injected into millions.</p>
<h2 id="the-covid-19-mrna-products">The COVID-19 mRNA Products</h2>
<h3 id="the-safety-narrative">The Safety Narrative</h3>
<p>The COVID-19 mRNA vaccines were declared "safe and effective" with an intensity and uniformity of messaging unprecedented in pharmaceutical history. Government agencies, media organizations, technology platforms, and healthcare institutions spoke with one voice: the vaccines were safe, side effects were mild and temporary, and anyone questioning the safety was spreading "misinformation."</p>
<p>The reality documented by researchers including Ana Mihalcea, MD, PhD (anamihalceamdphd.substack.com) stands in stark contrast to the safety narrative:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Self-assembling nanotechnology</strong> found in vaccine vials and human blood -- undisclosed, untested, unapproved</li>
<li><strong>SV40 cancer-causing DNA</strong> found in vaccine products -- undisclosed, exceeding regulatory limits by orders of magnitude</li>
<li><strong>Massive adverse event signals</strong> in VAERS, the Yellow Card system, and other reporting databases -- dismissed, minimized, or attributed to other causes</li>
<li><strong>Excess mortality</strong> in highly vaccinated populations -- unexplained, uninvestigated, and unacknowledged by health authorities</li>
<li><strong>Cardiac events, neurological damage, cancer acceleration</strong> -- documented by thousands of clinicians but denied by the agencies that approved the products</li>
</ul>
<p>The government assurance of safety for mRNA products follows the identical pattern established by DDT, thalidomide, Agent Orange, Vioxx, and SV40-contaminated polio vaccines. The assurance is emphatic. The evidence of harm is suppressed. The damage accumulates. And the truth will emerge -- not because institutions choose transparency, but because the harm becomes undeniable.</p>
<h3 id="regulatory-capture">Regulatory Capture</h3>
<p>The reason government safety assurances are worthless is structural: the regulatory agencies responsible for evaluating safety are captured by the industries they regulate.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>The FDA</strong> receives approximately 45% of its budget from pharmaceutical industry user fees. The industry funds the agency that regulates it.</li>
<li><strong>CDC advisory committee members</strong> have documented financial conflicts of interest with vaccine manufacturers. The individuals who recommend vaccine adoption have financial relationships with the companies that produce them.</li>
<li><strong>The revolving door</strong> between regulatory agencies and pharmaceutical companies means that the officials evaluating drug safety today are the industry executives of tomorrow (and vice versa).</li>
<li><strong>Classification and national security authorities</strong> shield pharmaceutical products developed under military contracts from normal regulatory scrutiny. Products deployed under Emergency Use Authorization bypass standard safety testing.</li>
</ul>
<p>Regulatory capture means that the agencies exist to serve the industry, not the public. Their safety assurances reflect the interests of the regulated, not the regulators. And the public, trusting these institutions, accepts products that the institutions have been paid to approve.</p>
